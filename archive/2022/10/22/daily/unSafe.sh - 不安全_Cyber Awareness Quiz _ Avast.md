How much do you know about cybersecurity? This five cyber awareness question quiz will help protect everyone in your family, from the baby to Grandma.

Take [our cyber awareness quiz](http://avastcyberquiz.com/) and learn how you can navigate discussions with your family around  the complexities of technology and digital threats. Our goal is help you, the "sandwich generation," keep both both aging parents and young children safe online.

[![cyber awareness quiz](https://blog.avast.com/hs-fs/hubfs/cyber%20awareness%20quiz.jpg?width=538&height=997&name=cyber%20awareness%20quiz.jpg)](http://avastcyberquiz.com/)

Parents today are striving to keep up with the fluency of their digitally native children while protecting them from myriad threats – cyberbullying, illegal activities, scams, grooming, etc.

And then there's the online safety of elderly parents. Keeping them safe means monitoring parents’ online activity and protecting them from scams, while still helping them maintaining their sense of independence and digital freedom.

Being a part of this “sandwich” generation is no easy feat. But maintaining a safe online digital identity is important at every age– and you can do it.
