We at Infosec Writeups are happy to announce we recently crossed 26k followers on our [publication](https://infosecwriteups.com/latest) and 20k followers on our [Twitter](https://twitter.com/InfoSecComm).

As always, we are overwhelmed with the love you have showered upon us. As our ways of giving back to the community, here are the two initiative we launched.

## Version 2.0 of Cybersecurity Conference IWCON 2022

In February 2022, we organized our [**first-ever virtual conference cum networking event**](https://medium.com/bugbountywriteup/check-out-the-full-speaker-line-up-of-iwcon-2022-6f62aa747420)**.** The response we got was beyond what we could have imagined. [Twitter was bursting with hashtags of IWCON2022](https://twitter.com/search?q=iwcon2022&src=typed_query).

**Some stats about the event**

* Total registrations: 700+.
* Total attendees: 400+.
* There were some sessions where the total no. of live attendees was 200+.

[**Now, we’re organizing the next version of IWCON 2022 on 17–18 December 2022**](https://iwcon.live/)**.** Some of our amazing talks are -

## Got any questions? We’re here to address them.

If you have any questions, doubts, or blockers stopping you from being a part of IWCON 2022, we’ll be happy to answer them.

Leave a reply to this email (or drop a comment below) and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

If you have a story of how you made a mark in Infosec and you’d like to speak at IWCON 2022, fill out our speakers form [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScotnPH8VWYGUW9UPFyA0CNIYh0asVrkeSeNXka736psVT9tg/viewform?usp=sf_link). We’d be happy if Infosec Writeups can be a stepping stone in your journey of personal branding.

Looking forward to seeing you at IWCON 2022.
