# Destination Linux Cybersecurity Interview with Craig Rowland

11 March 2025

Podcast

Sandfly founder Craig Rowland joins the [Destination Linux Podcast](https://tuxdigital.com/podcasts/destination-linux/) to talk about a wide range of cybersecurity topics such as:

* Working in cybersecurity from the BBS/Arpanet days to today.
* Life lessons from dumpster diving.
* Working in start-ups and what makes them more likely to be successful.
* How small errors leave networks vulnerable to attack.
* The myth of Linux security.
* Should you run anti-virus on Linux?
* Clever Linux hacks.
* The emerging role of AI in cybersecurity.

Craig's career spans more than 30 years in the vulnerability assessment and intrusion detection industry. We hope you enjoy this interview where he shares his stories and experiences and how they relate to Linux threats. We're happy to be a sponsor of Destination Linux and encourage you to subscribe.
