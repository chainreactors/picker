Hey everyone!

We are almost there: the **SAP TechEd in 2022** is just around the corner!

[SAP TechEd](https://www.sap.com/about/events/teched.html) is THE premier tech conference for all developers, IT practitioners, professionals, tech visionaries, innovators, and leaders who are interested in gaining a detailed understanding of SAP’s portfolio of solutions, platforms, and technologies, as well as insights into their future direction.

This year, SAP TechEd is going **hybrid** - for the first time!

The event will consist of an in-person and face-to-face, **paid** event in *The Mirage* in Las Vegas and a global, **free**, virtual event.

## **KEYNOTES**

**Attend the Opening** **Keynote with Jürgen Müller [KEY100]**

[**Executive Keynote**, 90 min]
Hear from Chief Technology Officer Juergen Mueller how you can thrive in times of global disruption and environmental challenges, and learn from SAP’s developer advocates how easy it is to succeed with SAP technology. Community focused. Customer driven.

Scheduled on Tuesday, Nov 15 | 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM CET (09:00 - 10:30 PST)

**Attend the Business Applications** **Keynote with Thomas Saueressig [KEY101]**

[**Executive Keynote**, 60 min]
This keynote is a comprehensive glance at our product portfolio. Learn how our business applications work together to help connect fragmented processes and data end to end – the base to maneuver in today’s new normal of constant crisis management.

Scheduled on Tuesday, Nov 16 | 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM CET (09:00 - 10:00 PST)
