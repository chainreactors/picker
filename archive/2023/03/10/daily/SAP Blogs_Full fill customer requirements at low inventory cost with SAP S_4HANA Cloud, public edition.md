* Do you have the situation that you have too little stock of the products you need?

* Do you have the situation that you have too much stock of the products you don't need?

* Do you want to reach the best possible customer service levels at lowest possible inventory costs?

If you have answered "Yes" to any of these questions, we invite you to join the sessions organized by **SAP Intelligent Adoption and Consumption Team** in collaboration with Product Management, on Demand Driven MRP in SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition.

Demand Driven MRP is relevant for a broad range of industries and applied to different business scenarios. The SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition offers an end-to-end process to help you realize demand driven MRP.![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/02/ValueGIF.gif)
