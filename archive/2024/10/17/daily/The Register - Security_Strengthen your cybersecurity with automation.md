Webinar In an era of ever-evolving cyber threats, staying ahead of potential security risks is essential.

For IT professionals, it's not just about working harder - it's about working smarter. That's where Google Security Operations comes in.

We invite you to join the Google Cloud webinar - [TechByte: Work Smarter, Not Harder with Google Security Operations](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/work-smarter-gso?utm_source=TheRegister&utm_medium=SponsoredContent&utm_campaign=Q4-emea-EMEA3471-onlineevent-er-dgcsm-work-smarter-GSO-oct&utm_content=TheRegister) - on 23 October 2024 at 10:30am BST to learn more. This session will explore how Google's advanced tools can help streamline your security processes while boosting effectiveness.
