## Too Long; Didn't Read

Apple's monopoly over smartphones extends beyond app distribution, encompassing strategies like limiting cross-platform technologies and leveraging subscription services. Their tactics not only stifle innovation but also increase consumer dependence on Apple products across various industries, including automotive infotainment systems.
