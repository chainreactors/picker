As mentioned in the Recap Blog Post [Recap of SAP Inside track Hyderabad (It’s Just the Beginning) | SAP Blogs](https://blogs.sap.com/2023/05/29/recap-of-sap-inside-track-hyderabad-its-just-the-beginning/) it’s just the beginning of Hyderabad SAP Community.

I would like to conclude [SAP Inside Track Hyderabad 2023](https://blogs.sap.com/2023/04/05/sap-inside-track-hyderabad-2nd-week-of-may/)by thanking all the organizers, volunteers, speakers, and sponsors with some of the highlights.

With 130+ Attendees including 35+ Customers & Partners like Deloitte, AWS, IBM, TCS, Capgemini, PepsiCo, and Henkel made the #sitHYD gathering was Incredible.

In continuation of the blog post, we would like to share our speaker's presentation documents in this Blog.
