# 报名即将截止！ICANN亚太地区域名系统论坛：域名系统功能和安全特性的演进发展，7月15日与您相约北京！

网安国际

**Pre-Event Webinar for ICANN APAC DNS Forum 2024**

自诞生以来，域名系统已运行长达40年之久，成为国际互联网的关键基础设施之一。域名系统的功能和安全特性经历了持续的演进发展：新型功能扩展和资源记录类型得到设计和应用，新的滥用模式和安全问题也频繁涌现。近年来，各界围绕域名系统新功能特性的部署监测、新安全风险的挖掘评估，开展了大量的研究工作。本场分论坛邀请国内外嘉宾分享关于该话题的最新研究成果，共同探讨域名系统的演进发展和解决方案。

**主题：**域名系统功能和安全特性的演进发展

  Evolving Operations, Abuse, and Security of the DNS

**时间：**2024年7月15日（周一）16:00-17:00

**地点：**奇安信安全中心（北京市西城区西直门外南路26号院1号楼）一层报告厅

**Stephen McQuistin**

**(University of St Andrews)**

Stephen McQuistin is an assistant professor at the University of St Andrews. He works on projects looking to improve the Internet protocol standardization process and its outputs, by designing tools and interventions that address both technical and social challenges.

**Yevheniya Nosyk**

**(Université Grenoble Alpes)**

Yevheniya Nosyk is a Ph.D. student at Université Grenoble Alpes, where she does research on DNS, network measurements, and cybersecurity to build a safer Internet for everyone. She is a recipient of the IRTF Applied Networking Research Prize (ANRP) 2024 for her work on Extended DNS Errors.

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/icelxY6ibIXSX5ev5QOA1Xnhz3xIicdIWO22t2NcJ1Haoiamm9GLz8rSsa4ibrUL0pqtI8WGdfibwrThezhsMpiaGiaEgg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

**Chendi Ma**

**(China Academy of Information and Communications Technology)**

Ma Chendi is an engineer at the Industrial Internet and Internet of Things Institute of China Academy of Information and Communications Technology, primarily focuses on DNS security and in-depth data mining and analysis. She has been involved in multiple national-level and ministerial-level construction projects. As a core member, Ma has also participated in projects under the National Key Research and Development Program of China with the theme of “Cyberspace Security Governance”. Ma boasts extensive experience in government work support, project construction, research topics, and report writing in the DNS field. Additionally, Ma holds over ten patents for inventions, software copyrights, and academic papers.

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/icelxY6ibIXSX5ev5QOA1Xnhz3xIicdIWO2eC18DF03NnjgOHxso7O6TedS4UtbhD3YlwSDf5MaqXml7mhia1RdEicg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

**Yunyi Zhang**

**(Tsinghua University)**

Yunyi Zhang is a Ph.D. student as a member of joint Ph.D. program between National University of Defense Technology and Tsinghua University. His research interests include network security, Internet measurement, and emerging cyberspace security threats. He has published papers at top academic venues, including USENIX Security, IMC, and TDSC.

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/icelxY6ibIXSX5ev5QOA1Xnhz3xIicdIWO2T92bFIxCRG3ok0S9icrXgveCT2QyO5jawcm4VDylTcLmGbjv0DQvDicg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

**Mingxuan Liu**

**(Zhongguancun Laboratory)**

Mingxuan Liu, Assistant Researcher of Zhongguancun Lab, received her PhD degree from Tsinghua University in 2023. Her main research interests are Internet infrastructure security analysis and cybercrime governance based on data-driven methodology. She has published papers at top academic venues and accumulated high-risk vulnerability reports. Her academic research results have achieved real-world impact by receiving bonus from several vendors, and the research results are adopted by well-known vendors such as Baidu, Tencent, Huawei, and Qi-anxin.

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/icelxY6ibIXSX5ev5QOA1Xnhz3xIicdIWO2YrEV1IbkvqSGrDqDtN3LScA4CU0EiaFFiaChic6yHyxVK8icbKTG0m3jXw/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

**Chaoyi Lu**

**(Tsinghua University)**

Chaoyi Lu is a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute for Network Sciences and Cyberspace, Tsinghua University. His research area lies in network security and Internet measurement, with particular interests in the DNS and other infrastructure. He has published papers at top academic venues and is a recipient of IRTF Applied Networking Research Prize (ANRP).
