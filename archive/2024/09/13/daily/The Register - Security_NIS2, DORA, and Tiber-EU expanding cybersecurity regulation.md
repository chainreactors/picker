Webinar As cybersecurity threats evolve, so do the regulations designed to protect businesses.

The latest frameworks - NIS2, DORA, and Tiber-EU - bring significant changes that require more than traditional compliance approaches.

For cybersecurity leaders, the challenge is clear: understanding and implementing these regulations effectively is key to safeguarding your organization. With global markets in play, staying ahead of these changes is crucial.

[Join us](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/30374/cybersecurity-regulation-steps-up-with-nis2-dora-and-tiber-eu) on September 16, 2024, at 11:00am BST (12:00pm CEST) for a critical webinar led by Chris Dale, SANS Principal Instructor. Chris will provide:

*- Comprehensive analysis* of NIS2, DORA, and Tiber-EU and their impact on IT security.

- *Strategic advice* on how these regulations will reshape the cybersecurity landscape.

- *Practical guidance* on best practices for achieving cyber resilience.

- *Exclusive insights* from the latest SANS Survey on readiness for NIS2.

This is your chance to ensure your cybersecurity strategy is aligned with the latest regulatory requirements. Don't miss this opportunity to gain actionable insights and prepare your organization for the future.
