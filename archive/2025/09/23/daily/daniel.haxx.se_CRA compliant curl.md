# CRA compliant curl

[September 22, 2025](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2025/09/22/cra-compliant-curl/) [Daniel Stenberg](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/author/daniel/) [Leave a comment](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2025/09/22/cra-compliant-curl/#respond)

As the [Cyber Resilience Act](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyber_Resilience_Act) (CRA) is getting closer and companies wanting to sell digital services in goods within the EU need to step up, tighten their procedures, improve their documentation and get control over their dependencies I feel it could be timely to remind everyone:

We of course offer full support and fully CRA compliant curl versions to [support customers](https://curl.se/support.html).

curl is not a *manufacturer* as per the legislation’s terminology so we as a project don’t have those requirements, but we always have our ducks in order and we will gladly assist and help *manufacturers* to comply.

We have done internet transfers for the world for decades. Fast, securely, standards compliant, feature packed and rock solid. We make curl to empower the world’s digital infrastructure.

You can rely on us.
