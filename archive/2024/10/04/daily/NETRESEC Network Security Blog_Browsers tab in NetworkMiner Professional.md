## [Browsers tab in NetworkMiner Professional](/?page=Blog&month=2024-10&post=Browsers-tab-in-NetworkMiner-Professional)

[![

](https://media.netresec.com/images/NetworkMiner-Professional-Tutorial_Browsers-Tab_1280x720.png)](https://media.netresec.com/videos/NetworkMiner-Professional-Tutorial_Browsers-Tab_1280x720.mp4)

The Browsers tab is a unique feature only available in [NetworkMiner Professional](https://www.netresec.com/?page=BuyNetworkMiner).
The PCAP files analyzed in this video are [pwned-se\_150312\_outgoing.pcap](https://media.netresec.com/pcap/pwned-se_150312_outgoing.pcap) and [pwned-se\_150312\_incoming.pcap](https://media.netresec.com/pcap/pwned-se_150312_incoming.pcap), which are snippets of the 4.4 GB [Hands-on Network Forensics dataset](https://share.netresec.com/s/nF5zNcaXLgwdQFZ) from FIRST 2015 ([slides](https://share.netresec.com/s/NBbonFnsZ4HqCTW)).

More information about NetworkMiner Professional's Browsers tab can be found in our blog post [Analyzing Web Browsing Activity](https://netresec.com/?b=162ef93).

See our [NetworkMiner Professional tutorial videos](https://www.netresec.com/?page=TutorialNMP) for additional tips and hints.
