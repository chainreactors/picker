## International Pizza Chain Domain Spoofing Report

![](https://bfore.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/pizza-image-1024x640.png)

## Overview

BforeAI researchers uncovered a sophisticated phishing campaign targeting well-known international pizza restaurant chains, resulting in financial losses. The investigation was initiated following a tip-off from a Singaporean police phishing advisory that was referenced in a [Yahoo News Singapore article](https://sg.news.yahoo.com/phishing-scam-dominos-pizza-customers-loss-seven-victims-singapore-030353039.html). The initial indicator of compromise (IOC) led to the discovery of a much larger, more elaborate and still active malicious campaign that spans multiple countries and targets various pizza restaurant brands.
