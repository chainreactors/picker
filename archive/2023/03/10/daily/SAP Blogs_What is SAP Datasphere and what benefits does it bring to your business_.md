### Introduction:

Organizations that have access to data and use it effectively, can become more agile and competitive. By understanding their data, they can develop innovative strategies to increase their profits, create new revenue streams and make better decisions. With the help of data-driven insights, organizations can also identify opportunities for improvement in their processes and operations. This will enable them to create a competitive advantage over other organizations that are not utilizing the power of their data. Therefore, it is important for organizations to fully harness the power of their data in order to remain competitive in today's market.

### Challenge:

Organizations may have their data landscape distributed among different applications and systems both in the cloud and on-premise.

Organizations spend countless resources extracting, rebuilding, and integrating their data.

### Solution:

SAP Datasphere is a comprehensive data service built on SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP) that enables to deliver seamless and scalable access to mission critical business data.

It provides a unified experience for :

* Data integration

* Data cataloging

* Semantic modeling

* Data warehousing

* Data federation

* Data virtualization

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/03/SAP-Datasphere-1.png)

### Benefits:

* Allows to integrate with other data vendors and external data products, bringing together various data sources to better understand the business data and make confident decisions based on real-time information

* Empower business users with data that is reliable and authoritative without needing to
  move all your data to a new system

* Allows users to easily enrich their data with information from multiple systems

* Self-service data enrichment for synergy between SAP and non-SAP data

Hands-on experience with SAP Datasphere through the SAP BTP free tier model

*Note: The SAP Datasphere instance is available for 90 days. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time during the 90 days. Free tier instances will be deleted after 90 days.*
