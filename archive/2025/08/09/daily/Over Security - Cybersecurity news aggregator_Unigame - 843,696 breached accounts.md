# Unigame

## What Happened

In December 2019, the now defunct gaming website Unigame (maker of Hunter Online) suffered a data breach [that was later redistributed as part of a larger corpus of data](https://cybernews.com/security/billions-passwords-credentials-leaked-mother-of-all-breaches/). The data included 844k email addresses and salted MD5 password hashes.
