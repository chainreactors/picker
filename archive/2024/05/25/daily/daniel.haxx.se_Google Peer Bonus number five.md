# Google Peer Bonus number five

[May 24, 2024](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2024/05/24/google-peer-bonus-number-five/) [Daniel Stenberg](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/author/daniel/)

It is not quite a [gold medal](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2017/10/20/my-night-at-the-museum/), but it is now the **fifth time** I have the honor of receiving a [Google Open Source Peer Bonus](https://opensource.google/documentation/reference/growing/peer-bonus). I might soon start to think I have some fans over there.

There is a monetary component to this bonus. Last time it was to the amount of 500 USD. I have not seen the amount for this time as it has not been transferred to me yet. I trust it will buy me a few good beers anyway.

![](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/OS-Peer-Bonus-H1-2024-Daniel-Stenberg-OSPB-Award-Letter.png)

**Update June 10:** it was 500 USD this time as well.
