### Jump to

### **Announcing the New Episode of Fusion Fireside**

**Featuring Senan Moloney, Global Head of Cybercrime and Cyber Fraud Fusion, Barclays**

Tune in to the latest episode of our Fusion Fireside series! Who better to discuss Fusion than Senan Moloney, the Global Head of Cybercrime and Cyber Fraud Fusion at Barclays? Senan has demonstrated that connecting threat intelligence with fraud prevention is not just a concept, it is a valuable operational practice.

In this episode, host Eward Driehuis and Senan discuss the intricacies of cybercrime and the strategies being employed to combat it. They recently spoke about the Fraud Kill Chain at the RSA Conference in San Francisco, inviting the Fraud and Fusion communities to collaborate and share structured information on the evolving landscape of financial crime.

Today, they continue the conversation and deep dive into the backgrounds of this effort.

---

### About Senan Moloney

Senan Moloney is the Global Head of Cybercrime and Cyber Fraud Fusion at Barclays. He leads the bank’s Cyber Fraud Fusion Centre, which focuses on intelligence-led defences against fraud and financial crime. The centre works to disrupt the upstream cyber enablers behind these crimes and invests in research and development to uncover emerging criminal attack paths.

Prior to joining Barclays, Senan was a senior cybercrime specialist at the European Cybercrime Centre (EC3) at Europol, supporting international investigations into organised cybercriminal groups. He is a recognised industry speaker on transforming fraud and financial crime strategies, and advises several cross-sector bodies tackling economic crime at a national and societal level.

This episode of Fusion Fireside offers a unique look into the challenges and innovations shaping the fight against cybercrime—featuring insights from two of the industry's leading minds.

---

### What to Expect in this Episode

Eward and Senan discuss:

* Senan’s background in law enforcement and his unexpected path to Europol
* How law enforcement experience adds value in fraud prevention
* The reality of leading a real-world Cyber Fraud Fusion team
* The challenges of navigating differing taxonomies and terminology
* The present and future of fraud through the lens of Senan’s team

Stay tuned for an insightful and thought-provoking discussion packed with practical takeaways for professionals working in fraud prevention, cyber threat intelligence, and financial crime.
