*Update (1/6/2025): The UN General Assembly on Dec. 24, 2024, adopted the UN Cybercrime Treaty without a vote. The treaty will open for signature at a formal ceremony to be hosted in Hanoi, Viet Nam, in 2025. It will enter into force 90 days after being ratified by the 40th signatory.*

Most UN Member States, [including the U.S](https://usun.usmission.gov/explanation-of-position-of-the-united-states-on-the-adoption-of-the-resolution-on-the-un-convention-against-cybercrime-in-ungas-third-committee/)., are expected to support adoption of the flawed UN Cybercrime Treaty when it’s scheduled to go before the UN General Assembly this week for a vote, despite warnings that it poses dangerous risks to human rights.
