# Secure your AI initiatives

## Unlock the power of generative AI with AWS

[Annaliese Ingrams](/Author/Annaliese-Ingrams "Read more by this author")

Thu 10 Oct 2024  //
14:16 UTC

![](/design_picker/d2e337b97204af4aa34dda04c4e5d56d954b216f/graphics/icons/social_share_icon.svg)

Webinar Generative AI (GenAI) has quickly transitioned from an emerging concept to a core driver of innovation across lots of different industries.

But as organizations integrate this transformative technology, security remains a top priority. So how can they accelerate their AI initiatives while ensuring robust protection for their data and systems?

Join Anna McAbee, Senior Solutions Architect, Security at AWS at [9am PDT, 12pm EDT, 4pm GMT on 29 October](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/31893/securing-generative-ai-fast-track-innovation-with-aws) for an in-depth exploration of security strategies tailored to GenAI. In this webinar, Anna will discuss how organizations can adapt their identity, access, and data privacy policies to secure AI workloads, while leveraging AWS tools such as Amazon Bedrock and Amazon Q.

Attendees will learn practical strategies for adopting generative AI while maintaining resilience, governance, and compliance best practices. This is an opportunity for IT leaders to gain actionable insights and explore the intersection of AI and security with a trusted industry expert.

Click [here](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/31893/securing-generative-ai-fast-track-innovation-with-aws) to secure your spot and take your AI projects to the next level.
