## Highlighted changes

* Add support for [[OKX.us](http://okx.us/)](<http://OKX.us>) - the US subsidiary of OKX.
* Add support for Bitget spot trading (including stoploss on exchange support).
* Update ta-lib to 0.6.5 - with a **lot** simpler installation.
* Improved `--backtest-directory` handling by splitting `--export-filename` into 2 separate arguments to avoid ambiguity.
* Improved `MarketCapPairList` to also support 1000PEPE (binance) and KPEPE like coins.
* Support Post Only orders on Binance futures.
* FreqUI
  + Fix a few backtesting layout issues
