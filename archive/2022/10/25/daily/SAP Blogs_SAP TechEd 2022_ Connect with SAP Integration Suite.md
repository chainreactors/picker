Are you curious about SAP's integration strategy and SAP Integration Suite sessions at SAP TechEd 2022?

My colleague emily.mui gives you a first introduction:

This blog post provides you an overview of all **SAP Integration Suite** sessions at **SAP TechEd 2022**, which our Product Management team of SAP Integration Suite and colleagues offer.

[SAP TechEd 2022](https://go3.events.sap.com/sapteched/hybrid/2022/reg/flow/sap/saptech2022/virtualdashboard/page/overview) is SAP’s premier hybrid event for developers, IT practitioners, professionals, and innovators. This year, TechEd 2022 will be again **open to everyone**as a 2-day hybrid event, November 15 -16th, 2022. SAP TechEd gives us a chance to connect with all of you to discuss exciting topics and gather feedback. Don’t forget to [register](https://go3.events.sap.com/sapteched/hybrid/2022/reg/flow/sap/saptech2022/registration/page/welcome)for this event and sign up for the sessions!

## **Lectures and Roadmap sessions**

[**SAP Integration Suite – Overview, Recent Innovations, and Road Map [IN203]**](https://go3.events.sap.com/sapteched/hybrid/2022/reg/flow/sap/saptech2022/sapteched2022catalog/page/catalog/session/1661198036478001EaEZ)

SAP Integration Suite is SAP’s enterprise integration platform-as-a-service for integrating heterogeneous system landscapes. It supports the integration of APIs, processes, data, and events. Learn about the key capabilities, recent innovations, and road map of SAP Integration Suite.

Speaker: lenamika (SAP), udo.paltzer(SAP), deschrijverp (delaware Consulting CV)

**Tuesday, Nov 15th, 11:00 - 11:25 PST (20:00 - 20:25 CET)**

Level: Advanced
Programming Language: Not Applicable to Session
Job Role: Architect, Consultant, Developer, Product Manager
