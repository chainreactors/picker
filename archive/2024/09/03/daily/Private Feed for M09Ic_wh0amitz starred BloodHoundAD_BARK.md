# BARK

[![BARK Logo](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/6fc324a32954cd56859d7a6341166e2d657c5c798782657f25454cb3e1c288a8/68747470733a2f2f692e696d6775722e636f6d2f736b504c4f37552e6a7067)](https://camo.githubusercontent.com/6fc324a32954cd56859d7a6341166e2d657c5c798782657f25454cb3e1c288a8/68747470733a2f2f692e696d6775722e636f6d2f736b504c4f37552e6a7067)

BARK stands for BloodHound Attack Research Kit. It is a PowerShell script built to assist the BloodHound Enterprise team with researching and continuously validating abuse primitives. BARK currently focuses on Microsoft's Azure suite of products and services.
