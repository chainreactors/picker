In today’s world, organizations are adapting to the future of work, with critical work taking place fluidly between corporate branches, home offices or on the road. Digital transformation initiatives are no longer isolated projects and are becoming a source of growth in a rapidly changing global marketplace. And, enterprises are exploring how they can leverage the power of AI to transform their operations with enhanced automation and increased productivity.

[![](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/word-image-327441-2.png)](https://www.saseconverge.paloaltonetworks.com/)

Join us for [SASE Converge](https://www.saseconverge.paloaltonetworks.com/) 2024, a 2-hour immersive virtual experience, featuring thought leaders and technologists from across the globe. We will reveal how AI-powered Prisma® SASE raises the bar on security while transforming IT and providing exceptional end-user experiences. You’ll learn how the latest advancements in SASE drive meaningful results:

* Easily extend consistent security to all devices with the industry's only SASE solution that secures work on managed or unmanaged devices.
* Ensuring 5X faster than direct-to-internet performance for better app experiences with a growing ecosystem of applications.
* Safely enabling GenAI apps for the workforce.
* Delivering a streamlined IT experience through transformational simplicity and automation.

[![Register Now button](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/word-image-327441-3.png)](https://www.saseconverge.paloaltonetworks.com/)

Palo Alto Networks continues to be an innovator and leader in transforming digital enterprises through security, networking and IT operations with Prisma SASE. Recently, Palo Alto Networks was recognized as a [Leader in 2024 Gartner® Magic Quadrant™ for Single-Vendor SASE](https://start.paloaltonetworks.com/gartner-sase-mq-2024.html) and a Leader in [The Forrester Wave™: Security Service Edge Solutions, Q1 2024](https://start.paloaltonetworks.com/forrester-sse-report.html) reports.

[Register now](https://www.saseconverge.paloaltonetworks.com/) to hear all about the last innovations in AI-powered SASE. We look forward to seeing you there!
