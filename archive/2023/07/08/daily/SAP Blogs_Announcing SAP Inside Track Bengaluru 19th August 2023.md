Get ready for the most highly anticipated SAP community event of the year! We are thrilled to announce the upcoming SAP Inside Track Bengaluru on August 19th, 2023. Following the tremendous success of our earlier event ([Blog Post Link](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/11/08/announcing-sap-inside-track-in-bengaluru-2023-sitblr2023/)), we are excited to bring together the SAP community once again for an extraordinary day filled with learning, networking, and collaboration. Also get a sneak peek into the upcoming TechEd 2023 which is scheduled for Nov 2-3 here in Bengaluru!

**What is SAP Inside Track?**

SAP Inside Track is an informal, community-driven event where SAP professionals, developers, consultants, architects, and leaders come together to share their knowledge and insights on various SAP-related topics. This grassroots initiative, organized by members of the SAP Community, provides a platform for fostering connections, staying updated on the latest SAP technologies, and gaining recognition for your technical expertise.

[Know more about SAP Inside Track Here!](https://groups.community.sap.com/t5/welcome-corner-blog-posts/sap-inside-track-what-is-it-about/ba-p/129189)

## **Event Details:**

**Date:** August 19th, 2023
**Time:** 8 AM - 5 PM IST
**Venue:** SAP Labs India, 138, SAP Labs Rd, EPIP Zone, Whitefield, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560066

## **Registration:**

Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to be part of the largest SAP community event in Bengaluru. Register now for SAP Inside Track Bengaluru on August 19th, 2023.

[Register Now!](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/sap-inside-track-bengaluru-august-19th-2023-tickets-672925065987?aff=oddtdtcreator)

**Note: This is an in-person event only.**

## **Agenda:**

We have an exciting agenda planned for this event. There will be a total of 9 tracks and a dedicated track for Hands-on on the latest SAP Technologies including but not limited to SAP BTP, Concur, UX, SAP S/4HANA, AI with SAP and Low Code/No Code. Follow the below blog post to get the latest agenda.

[Agenda Here!](https://groups.community.sap.com/t5/bengaluru-blog-posts/agenda-for-sap-inside-track-bengaluru-august-19th-2023/ba-p/270364)

## **Speaker Registration:**

Do you have valuable insights and experiences to share with the SAP community? We invite you to become a speaker at SAP Inside Track Bengaluru. Check out the below blog post for more information and nominate yourself as a speaker for the event

[Speaker Registration Here!](https://groups.community.sap.com/t5/bengaluru-blog-posts/call-for-speakers-sap-inside-track-bengaluru-2023-second-edition/ba-p/270165)

## **Join the Bengaluru SAP Community Group:**

If you're based in Bengaluru or anywhere in Karnataka, we invite you to join our vibrant Bengaluru SAP Community Group. Engage in meaningful discussions, share ideas, and collaborate with fellow SAP enthusiasts. Use the below link to join.

<https://groups.community.sap.com/t5/bengaluru/gh-p/bangalore>
