Registrations for the [SAP Inside Track Bengaluru 4th March 2023](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/11/08/announcing-sap-inside-track-in-bengaluru-2023-sitblr2023/) are officially open now! Join us on March 4th, 2023 for a jam-packed day of learning, networking & fun.

# When

Mark your calendars for March 4th, 2023 and be sure to arrive early for registration between 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM. The event will run from 9:30 AM – 5:00 PM, giving you a full day to immerse yourself in all things SAP.

# Location

SAP Labs Bengaluru is the perfect setting for this event, and we are grateful to have them as our venue sponsor. ([map](https://goo.gl/maps/TWLrPFuSLEZ4QkcU6))

# Agenda

Our agenda is jam-packed with sessions led by some of the foremost experts in the field. You'll have the opportunity to learn about the latest advancements in SAP Build Process Automation, Integration, Security, and many more as well as **8 tracks** focusing on specific areas of interest such as Application Development, Functional Areas, Low Code No Code, Design & SAP Cloud products. Plus, we're also working on making hands-on experience available with **Developers Space**, so you can apply your new knowledge in a practical setting.

[Check out our Agenda](https://groups.community.sap.com/t5/bengaluru-blog-posts/mark-your-calendars-for-sap-inside-track-bengaluru-2023-check/ba-p/221054) - kavya.krishnan

# Register Here

As this is an offline event with limited seating capacity, we kindly ask that you only register if you are certain that you will be able to attend. You will get a **confirmation mail** with instructions, check your mail spam folder.

**Tip**: use your personal mail id to register as the confirmation mail might go to spam if use your official company email.

[Link: Register now using Eventbrrite](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/sap-inside-track-bengaluru-feb-25th-2023-tickets-515431408217)
> ***Note**: In case your plans change and you are unable to attend, please unregister yourself in the eventbrite or let us know at sapcommunitybengaluru@gmail.com so that others on the waiting list can take your spot.*

**No Spot Registration**

So don't miss out on this opportunity to learn, share, and network with the best in the community. You can support this event by sharing this post on LinkedIn & Twitter and letting your colleagues know about it. We can't wait to see you there, don't let this opportunity pass you by!

Follow the [announcement post](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/11/08/announcing-sap-inside-track-in-bengaluru-2023-sitblr2023/), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sitblr/) and [Twitter](https://twitter.com/sitBLR) for more updates.
