SAP Integration and Certification Center (SAP ICC) would like to formally invite all partners with SAP-certified solutions and all Independent Software Vendors (ISVs) to our quarterly **Partner Meetup Event** on Wednesday**, May 3, 2023** at **3:00 PM - 6:30 PM** in **San Francisco, California**. Please join us in spending an afternoon full of interactive and open discussions with product and integration experts at **SAP Silicon Valley**.

In addition to networking and connecting with one another, this event is geared towards providing you with relevant product updates from SAP. Additionally, we would highly value your feedback on how we can improve the certification process and related services.
