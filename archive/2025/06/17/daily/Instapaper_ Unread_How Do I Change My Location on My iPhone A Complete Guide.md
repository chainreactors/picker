**Method 1: Use iPhone Settings to Manage Location Access**

Although iOS doesn’t allow you to directly set a fake location, you can still control how apps use your current GPS data. Here are the steps.

**Step 1:** Navigate to Settings, tap Privacy & Security, tap Location Services

**Step 2:** Scroll to choose the specific app (e.g., Maps, Instagram)

**Step 3:** Select Never, Ask Next Time, or While Using the App

**Step 4:** Toggle Precise Location off for more general location sharing

This method doesn’t let you manually pick a fake location; it simply limits apps’ access to your real location.
