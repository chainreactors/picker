Hello Everyone,

I'm Deepali and I’m the Portfolio Manager for SAP Sales Cloud training and enablement within the SAP Customer Experience Product Learning team.  We at SAP Learning are working on transforming our platform, content and focusing on creating Digital First, Role-Based E-Learning opportunities.

On behalf of the team, I am pleased to announce the official release of  [**Discover SAP Sales Cloud**](https://learning.sap.com/learning-journey/discover-sap-sales-cloud), a new eLearning on [Learning.sap.com](http://Learning.sap.com)

[![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/03/dic-sap-sales-clou.jpg)](https://learning.sap.com/learning-journey/discover-sap-sales-cloud)

Intended for **Sales Executives** or **Business Users** this FREE learning opportunity is delivered in an easy-to-learn format and self-paced manner, not only with text and graphical information, but also with **demo videos** and quizzes.

 This will include knowledge of sales business processes, including topics such as lead management, opportunity management, quotation management, sales contracts, Add ons and more

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2021/08/hat.png)  After completing this course and answering all the quiz questions correctly, you earn a **Record of Achievement** for yourself!

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/03/rec-of-ac.jpg)

Give a try! We plan to create more and more learning content for this area, and we need you to go over the lessons and let us know if they are useful for you or your team.

Subscribe to the  [Sales Cloud](https://microlearning.opensap.com/category/SAP%2BSales%2BCloud/173267271)    channels to get updates on the latest videos. |

I’ll be back soon with more learning resources related to SAP Sales Cloud.

In the mean time, I’m looking forward to your feedback and questions via comments in this post.

Happy Learning!
