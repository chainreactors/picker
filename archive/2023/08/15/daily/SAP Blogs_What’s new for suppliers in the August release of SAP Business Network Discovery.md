Starting on August 18th, SAP Ariba Discovery will be rebranded to “SAP Business Network Discovery”. To bring you a more connected experience for lead generation via the network, you can now access SAP Business Network Discovery directly from your Trading Partner Portal under the “Leads” tab.

In addition to the new name and user interface, this upcoming release introduces enhanced capabilities to help you get matched to better business opportunities, have more visibility over your leads from various sources, and sell faster:

* **Centralized experience** to browse through postings, respond to leads, and submit bids

* **Leverage a new “Leads’ dashboard** to manage and search through your open leads from various sources

* **Receive specific and relevant leads** with improved algorithms that help you qualify opportunities

* **Better control over notifications** by selecting which notifications to receive

* **Distinguish your company with specifications** **and credentials**, such as sustainability ratings, to let buyers who have specific partnership preferences find you more easily

**Accessibility information**

Please note that this release will occur in phases, beginning on August 18th. Once the release is enabled for your account, you will see the “Leads” tab in your SAP Business Network Trading Partner Portal.

To find more information about the release, click [here](https://support.ariba.com/item/view/178523) or check out this Wiki page on the [SAP Business Network Help Portal](https://help.sap.com/docs/ariba/business-network-wip/pay-12205-rfi-lifecycle-on-discovery-2-0?state=DRAFT) .
