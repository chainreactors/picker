Goal of improving customer experience is achieved with high product quality and services provided for the product throughout its lifecycle. Running efficient in-house repair process is one of key components to achieve that goal.

SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition offers “In-House Repair” business process, with its set of apps and functionalities to help you achieve this goal.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/11/Picture1-19.png)

Steps within In-House Repair Business Process in SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition

By adopting In-House Repairs, you can profit from following system capabilities:

* Creating, planning, and executing In-House Repairs of serialized devices and components

* Providing cost estimates to customers, if needed in the form of a repair quotation per device

* Initiate repair specific procurement process with order execution

* Post timesheet, cost of spares, and expenses automatically based on the repair confirmation

* Invoice your customers based on either the repair confirmations or the repair order (time and material or fixed-price)

* Integrate with Returns Management to provide inbound logistics for your customers equipment
