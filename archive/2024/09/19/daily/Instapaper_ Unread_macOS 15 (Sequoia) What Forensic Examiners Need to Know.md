# macOS 15 (Sequoia): What Forensic Examiners Need to Know

Apple’s latest macOS 15 (Sequoia) is packed with updates that could impact forensic investigations, especially for examiners dealing with Apple devices. With new privacy features, expanded functionality, and deeper AI integration, there’s a lot to unpack. Let’s break down the key changes and what they could mean for the future of your forensic work.

## macOS 15 Features That Matter to Digital Forensics

### Passwords as an App

**What’s New:** Apple has turned the Passwords tab into a full-fledged app. It works across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Vision Pro, and even Windows (via iCloud), offering password management, Passkeys, and two-factor authentication (2FA) codes.
