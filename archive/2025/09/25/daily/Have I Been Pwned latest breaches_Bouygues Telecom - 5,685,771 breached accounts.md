# Bouygues Telecom

## What Happened

In August 2025, [the French telecommunications company Bouygues Telecom detected a cyber attack against their services](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/bouygues-telecom-confirms-data-breach-impacting-64-million-customers/). The incident resulted in a data breach that exposed almost 6.4M customer records, including 5.7M unique email addresses. The breach also exposed names, physical addresses, phone numbers, dates of birth and IBANs (International Bank Account Numbers). Bouygues Telecom advised that all affected customers had been notified about the incident.
