**We are excited** **to feature the SAP Community Spotlight** this month and share why SAP Signavio enables and speeds up your continuous business process transformation.

In today's world, with rapid changes and market disruptions, companies must stay agile and transform fast. Not only to adapt their value chains and business operations but also to ensure excellent customer experience and remain relevant in the market. However, changing and adapting operations and processes quickly and at scale across the company can be challenging and time-consuming.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/02/Screenshot-2023-02-07-at-11.22.44.png)

That's where **SAP Signavio** can support: as an **end-to-end process management solution** it gives you the ability to understand, enhance, and transform all your business processes – fast and at scale with the help of all of your business users.

SAP Signavio provides you with everything you need to succeed in continuous business process transformation:

* A complete suite to support process transformation from analytics to execution

* A transformation methodology centered around people and collaboration

**And now:** a collection of highly consumable **value accelerators** that will drive "time to value" throughout your transformation journey.

<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rzGnM7_tHk8>

The main ingredients for a successful transformation

**Fast-time-to-insights**
SAP Signavio reduces the time to discover value potentials in your process analytics and mining initiatives, allowing you to find improvement areas faster and act based on concrete recommendations as well as industry-level benchmarks.

**Fast-time-to-adapt**
In addition, SAP Signavio allows you to model processes and journeys quickly based on best practice content, driving collaborative efforts under one common value and process governance.

Transforming successfully means that you quickly drive strategy to execution:

1. Translate business strategy into well-defined targets and optimization initiatives. Consume best practices to quickly scope changes and required system capabilities.

2. Leverage core system optimization as well as extensibility / low code automation capabilities for real end-to-end optimization

3. Put the business user in the driver's seat of continuous and sustainable transformation based on a collaborative approach

As your business strategy evolves continuously to meet your customer, suppliers, employees, and other demands, your operations and processes need to continuously be adapted.

You start or are already within your transformation, so please look at our guidance on which steps you should focus on during your [business process transformation mission](https://community.sap.com/topics/signavio/business-process-transformation).

**Stay tuned through this month** to learn everything you need to transform your business with SAP Signavio successfully.
