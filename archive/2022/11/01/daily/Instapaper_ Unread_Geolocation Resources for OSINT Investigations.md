# Geolocation Resources for OSINT Investigations

Oct 28 2022

There are numerous free tools and resources to help boost your geolocation and vehicle tracking skills to further your OSINT investigations.

Authored bySANS Institute

![SANS Institute](/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.contentstack.io%2Fv3%2Fassets%2Fbltabe50a4554f8e97f%2Fblt12dfb7ac1329de96%2F685a5d06e911caa59e2287ca%2FSANS-WEB_SANS-Profile-Page_Profile-Pic_504_x_504.jpg&w=1024&q=75)

On the [Lifting the Fog livestream series,](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CsPd8q4YFUo&list=PLs4eo9Tja8bhhRQCJUCui8b39BhKZQvgi) Steven discussed geolocation and vehicle tracking. They each shared helpful tools and resources they use to further their skills and OSINT investigations.

### Tools

* [InVid](https://www.invid-project.eu/) - Verify the reliability of video and image files
* [Google Lens](https://lens.google/) - Search what you see to identify things like dog breed, plant species, shoe make and designer, and more
* [Overpass Turbo](https://overpass-turbo.eu/) - Build interactive maps to help visualize location data
* [Mapillary](https://www.mapillary.com/) - Access street-level images and map data from around the world
* [KartaView](https://kartaview.org/) - Collect street-level imagery from around the world
* [CarNet](https://carnet.ai/) - Upload an image of a vehicle to determine its make, model, and year
* [WorldLicensePlates.com](http://worldlicenseplates.com/) - Research historical and current license plate designs from across the world
* [VehicleHistory.com](https://www.vehiclehistory.com/) - Search vehicle history based on VIN and license plate
* [Poctra](https://poctra.com/) - Search salvage and auctioned cars in the US and EU

### Resources

* [GeoGuessr](https://www.geoguessr.com/) - Build your skills in identifying landscapes, landmarks, and more
* [QuizTime](https://medium.com/quiztime) - Boost your geolocation and verification skills
