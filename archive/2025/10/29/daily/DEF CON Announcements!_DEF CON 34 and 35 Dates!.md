* + ### Start Here.

    New to DEF CON?
    Find out what it's all about!

    DEF CON has been a part of the hacker community for over three decades. Pick up a bit of history and origin by watching **[DEF CON - The Documentary](https://media.defcon.org/DEF%20CON%2020/DEF%20CON%2020%20documentary/DEF%20CON%2020%20Documentary%201080p%20x264.mp4)**, and by checking out the **[About Page](html/links/dc-about.html)**. The **[DEF CON FAQ](html/links/dc-faq/dc-faq.html)** has the answers to most of the common questions you might have.

* + ### Get Educated.

    A treasure trove of hacking knowledge
    awaits in our past media!

    ![logo for DEF CON Media server](images/defcon-main/dc-media.png)

    **[DEF CON Media Server](https://media.defcon.org/)**. Speeches, music, art, and more!

    ![Logo for DEF CON Torrents page](images/defcon-main/dc-torrents.png)

    For the big downloaders, **[check out our Torrents](html/links/dc-torrent.html)**.

    ![Logo for infocon.org](images/defcon-main/infocon-logo.webp)

    **[Infocon, the hacking con archive](https://infocon.org)**! Con video, podcasts, rainbow tables, and more!

* + ### Get Involved.

    Want to participate?
    There are a myriad of ways!

    DEF CON is what you make it. If you'd like to do more than just attend, have a look at our **[Get Involved page](html/links/get-involved.html)** for ideas!

    Year round, consider joining a local **[DEF CON Group](https://defcongroups.org/)**, or get active on the **[DEF CON Forums](https://forum.defcon.org/)**!

## DEF CON 34 and 35 Dates!

Posted 2025-10-29

![Calendar with DEF CON 34 and 35 dates displayed](images/defcon-main/post-images/3435-dates.webp)

Good News, Everyone! We have the official dates for DEF CON 34! And to make up for the delay, we also have the dates for DEF CON 35!

Please join us at the Las Vegas Convention Center August 6-9 in 2026 and August 5-8 in 2027.

Save the dates, friends. It'll be here before you know it.

See you there.
