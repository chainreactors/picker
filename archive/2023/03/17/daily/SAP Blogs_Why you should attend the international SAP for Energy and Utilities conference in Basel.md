As energy and utility companies become increasingly interconnected, they must stay up-to-date with technological advancements. The International SAP for Energy and Utilities provides a unique opportunity to do just this.

[International SAP Conference for Utilities and Energy - What is it about? - SAP Media Share](https://video.sap.com/media/t/1_ogl0f49n)

Organized by TAC and SAP, the world’s leading provider of The Intelligent Suite (S/4HANA) software, the International SAP for Energy and Utilities has a strong history of delivering insights and knowledge on using SAP and partner products. This conference provides a platform for attendees to stay current in this fast-moving industry.

At the International SAP for Energy and Utilities, attendees learn from leading industry leaders and experts who cover topics ranging from leveraging the latest SAP technology and analytics to improve customer experience and operational efficiency to digitalizing processes and using machine learning for data-driven decision-making. Additionally, companies that attend the International SAP for Energy and Utilities benefit from up-to-date product knowledge.
