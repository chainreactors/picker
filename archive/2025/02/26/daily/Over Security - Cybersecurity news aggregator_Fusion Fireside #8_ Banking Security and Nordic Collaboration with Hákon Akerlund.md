### **Announcing the New Episode of Fusion Fireside**

**Featuring Hákon Akerlund from Arion Banki**

We are thrilled to announce the latest episode of **Fusion Fireside**, hosted by our very own **Eward Driehuis**. This week, we have the pleasure of welcoming **Hákon Akerlund**, a distinguished figure from the Icelandic bank **Arion Banki**, to share his insights and experiences.

Hákon is renowned in the banking sector, with a wealth of experience spanning several decades. He has played a pivotal role in shaping Iceland’s financial landscape, particularly through his various positions at Arion Banki. His expertise in banking security, fraud prevention, and financial resilience has earned him numerous accolades and a reputation as a thought leader in the industry. Hakon’s career is defined by his commitment to fostering community and collaboration among Nordic banks.

## What to Expect in this Episode

* **Community Matters: The Importance of Nordic Collaboration**
* **A Unique Perspective: The Nordics vs the Rest of the World**
* **Close-Knit Connections: Iceland’s Unique Advantage**
* **Current Trends: Scams & Fraud Prevention**
* **Resilience Through Adversity: Impact of the Financial Crash**
* **Swift Recovery: Iceland’s Remarkable Rebound**
* **Banking Security: Hakon’s Journey**
* **Adventures in Banking: Tales from the Trenches**

We hope you tune in to this enlightening episode of Fusion Fireside and gain valuable insights from Hákon Akerlund’s experiences and expertise. Stay tuned for more exciting episodes as we continue to bring you fascinating conversations with industry leaders.
