## Virtual gunslingers forcibly became cheaters via mystery means

Updated Esports pros competing in the Apex Legends Global Series (ALGS) Pro League tournament were forced to abandon their match today due to a suspected cyberattack.

In the early hours of Monday morning, two professional Apex Legends players competing in two separate matches were forcibly given cheats on their accounts – events that have seen the tournament temporarily shut down.

Noyan Ozkose (whose alias is Genburten) of the Dark Zero team was suddenly given the ability to [see all opponents through walls](https://twitter.com/Apryze_/status/1769516394138206277), while Phillip Dosen (alias: ImperialHal) of the TSM team was also suddenly gifted the ability to [aim automatically at his opponents](https://twitter.com/ImperialHal/status/1769525888721313920) without any manual application to the controller – an "aimbot."

![gamers playing games](https://regmedia.co.uk/2017/04/05/esports.jpg?x=174&amp;y=115&amp;crop=1)
