Hello Reader,

Several other people have joined the Zelster challenge with me and its been great reading what they've been putting out. I thought it would be great today to spotlight one of them, Oleg Skulkin.

I've been following Oleg's work for many years now and have always enjoyed what he's written in the past, so it wasn't a surprise when I found his new blog [Know Your Adversary](https://www.knowyouradversary.ru/) to be just as good. In just the first four posts of the year Oleg has already put out some great posts and shared some valuable insights. If you've been reading my blog for a long time (817 posts in total as of this blog) then you should check out Oleg's as well.

I'll be tracking and highlighting other challenge participants through the year!

Speaking of challenges, tomorrow is Sunday! The Sunday Funday Challenge returns and I look forward to seeing what you all can do!
