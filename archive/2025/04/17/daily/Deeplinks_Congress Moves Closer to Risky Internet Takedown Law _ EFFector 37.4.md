Sorry, EFF doesn't hand out candy like the Easter Bunny, but we are here to keep you updated on the latest digital rights news with our EFFector newsletter!

This edition of EFFector explains how you can [help us push back](https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2025/04/congress-takes-another-step-toward-enabling-broad-internet-censorship) against the TAKE IT DOWN Act, an internet censorship law; why we oppose [site-blocking legislation](https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2025/04/congress-reviving-site-blocking-and-its-just-dangerous-ever), found in two upcoming bills; and how to [delete your data](https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2025/03/how-delete-your-23andme-data) from 23andMe.
