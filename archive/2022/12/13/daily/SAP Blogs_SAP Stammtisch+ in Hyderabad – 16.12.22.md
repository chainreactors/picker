Hello SAP Community members,

We are very excited to announce that the first-ever in-person SAP Stammtisch Plus Community event is happening in Hyderabad. We invite fellow community members from the Hyderabad region to join us on-site for a Stammtisch PLUS meet-up.

**What is a Stammtisch** – For those unfamiliar with Stammtisch, it is an informal meetup where SAP and non-SAP topics are discussed. There are also networking opportunities and plenty of fun. Additionally, if one or two formal sessions are added, it becomes Stammtisch PLUS (+).

For the upcoming Stammtisch PLUS meetup, we want to get to know each other and mobilize the community in the first place.

**Who all are invited**

Anyone and everyone interested in SAP Community topics, be it SAP users, developers, consultants, architects, or students – come, join us, and bring your friends too!

**Agenda**

1. **The Role Of Identity & Access Management In E2E Enterprise Solutions**Identity & Access Management is the backbone of enterprise solutions to enforce security and implement role based access control (RBAC) to the business applications in an E2E enterprise suite. It also enables and enhances the user experience with Single-Sign-On without having to login to each business application individually.

2. Community Speaker - YTD

**When & Where?**

On Friday 16-12-2022, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Location: Callidus Cloud, 6th floor, Building No. 12B, Raheja Mindspace IT Park

For registrations, [click here](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/sap-stammtisch-hyderabad-tickets-484329712207)

\*Refreshments are on the house!!
\*\* Please be at location 10min in prior for security check

Please follow this blog to stay posted on all the recent updates and information. If any queries feel free to post in the reply section.

Looking forward to seeing you all!!

Best Regards
