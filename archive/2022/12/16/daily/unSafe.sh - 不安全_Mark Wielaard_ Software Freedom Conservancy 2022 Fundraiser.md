## Software Freedom Conservancy 2022 Fundraiser

Posted on December 15, 2022, 11:22.

![](https://sfconservancy.org/videos/sfc-introduction-video_poster.jpg)

Please donate to the [Software Freedom Conservancy](https://sfconservancy.org/news/2022/nov/22/2022-fundraiser/) this year. *Software Freedom Conservancy has been growing and is able to take on the work it does thanks to the incredible support of individuals who care about an organization who stands up for the equitable, ethical and end user focused technologies.*

They have been a great partner to [Sourceware](https://sourceware.org/mission.html), helping with the GNU Toolchain Infrastructure, putting developers and community first.
