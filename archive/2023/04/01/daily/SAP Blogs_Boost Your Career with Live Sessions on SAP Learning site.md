Self-study is not always easy. Sometimes we feel stuck, lack motivation or simply struggle to find the answer to a burning question we have. To help you stay motivated, we are thrilled to announce the launch of our inaugural series of live sessions on SAP Learning site!

These live sessions will be offered as a pilot program free of charge, providing you with the support necessary to make the most of your learning experience - and in turn, boost your career. You will have access to Q&A sessions, demonstrations, and real-time support from instructors and fellow learners, all of which will help you maximize your SAP knowledge.

**Pick Your Type of Session**

There are two types of live sessions to choose from: Our Q&A live sessions provide you with the opportunity to attend scheduled sessions with instructors who can answer questions.Our expert deep dive live sessions allow you to explore complex topics that go beyond the self-paced digital learning content that you have already mastered.

**Developing Side-by-Side Extensions with SAP BTP**

* [Q&A Live Session: Developing Side-by-Side Extensions with SAP BTP](https://webinars.sap.com/series/qa-live-session-developing-side-by-side-extensions-with-sap-btp/en/seriesdetails)

* [Expert Deep Dive Live Session: Deploy a CAP application to SAP BTP, Kyma Runtime](https://webinars.sap.com/series/deploy-a-cap-application-to-sap-btp-kyma-runtime/en/seriesdetails)

**Developing with SAP Integration Suite**

* [Q&A Live Session: Developing with SAP Integration Suite](https://webinars.sap.com/series/qa-live-session-developing-with-sap-integration-suite/en/seriesdetails)

* [Expert Deep Dive Live Session: Create an API Provider and API Proxy based on an On-premise system](https://webinars.sap.com/series/create-an-api-provider-and-api-proxy-based-on-an-on-premise-syste/en/seriesdetails)

**SAP Build**

* [Expert Deep Dive Live Sessions for SAP Build](https://webinars.sap.com/series/expert-deep-dive-sessions-for-sap-build/en/seriesdetails)

**SAP Analytics Cloud**

* [Q&A Live Session: SAP Analytics Cloud – Story Design and Data Modelling](https://webinars.sap.com/series/qa-live-session-for-sap-analytics-cloud-story-design-and-data-mod/en/seriesdetails)

* [Expert Deep Dive Live Sessions for SAP Analytics Cloud](https://webinars.sap.com/series/expert-deep-dive-sessions-for-sap-analytics-cloud/en/seriesdetails)

**ABAP Development and ABAP on SAP BTP**

* [Q&A Live Session – ABAP Programming on SAP Business Technology Platform](https://webinars.sap.com/series/qa-live-session-abap-programming-on-sap-business-technology-platf/en/seriesdetails)

* [Expert Deep Dive Live Sessions for ABAP](https://webinars.sap.com/series/expert-deep-dive-sessions-for-abap/en/seriesdetails)

You can also access and register to these live sessions via our dedicated product overview pages on [SAP Analytics Cloud](https://learning.sap.com/products/business-technology-platform/data-analytics/analytics-cloud?url_id=text-sapcommunity-prdteng-LiveSessions-SAPAnalyticsCloud), [SAP Build](https://learning.sap.com/products/sap-build?url_id=text-sapcommunity-prdteng-LiveSessions-SAPBuildNoCode), and [SAP BTP](https://learning.sap.com/products/business-technology-platform/development/extensions?url_id=text-sapcommunity-prdteng-LiveSessions-ExtSAPBTP). But don’t worry, if you’re already in the middle of learning, you can also access the live sessions directly from the learning journeys:

  + [Build Side-by-Side Extensions with SAP BTP](https://learning.sap.com/learning-journey/build-side-by-side-extensions-on-sap-btp?url_id=text-sapcommunity-prdteng-LiveSessions-ExtSAPBTP)

  + [Compose and Automate with SAP Build the No-Code Way](https://learning.sap.com/learning-journey/compose-and-automate-with-sap-build-the-no-code-way?url_id=text-sapcommunity-prdteng-LiveSessions-SAPBuildNoCode)
