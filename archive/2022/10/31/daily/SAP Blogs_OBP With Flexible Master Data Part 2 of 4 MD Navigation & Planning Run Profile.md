This video is the second part of the series called SAP IBP OBP With Flexible Master Data. This video covers details of navigation for important Master Data like Supply Chain Dependency from the web UI along with PDS & source of supply. It also focuses on setting up and configuring Planning Run Profiles, based on different constraints and prioritization, that are needed to execute a supply planning run in SAP IBP Response & Supply, using OBP Optimizer & OBP Finite Heuristic.
