# Samsung Germany Customer Tickets

## What Happened

In March 2025, [data from Samsung Germany was compromised in a data breach of their logistics provider, Spectos](https://www.infostealers.com/article/samsung-tickets-data-leak-infostealers-strike-again-in-massive-free-dump/). Allegedly due to credentials being obtained by malware running on a Spectos employee's machine, the breach included 216k unique email addresses along with names, physical addresses, items purchased from Samsung Germany and related support tickets and shipping tracking numbers.
