[Sysinternals tools updates](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/sysinternals-blog/process-explorer-v17-0-handle-v5-0-process-monitor-v3-92-and/ba-p/3663465) have been released for

* [Process Explorer](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sysinternals/downloads/process-explorer) v17.0
* [Handle](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sysinternals/downloads/handle) v5.0
* [Process Monitor](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sysinternals/downloads/procmon) v3.92
* [Sysmon](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sysinternals/downloads/sysmon) v14.11

Make sure to update sysmon, as it includes a bug fix.

I like the update to the Handles and DLLs view: it's multitab now, making it easier to switch (unless you are used to the control keys to switch).
