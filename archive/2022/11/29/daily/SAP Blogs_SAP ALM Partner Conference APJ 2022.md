**Dear ALM Partners, Customers,**

It gives us an immense pleasure and privilege to express deep gratitude towards your support in making the “SAP ALM Partner Conference APJ 2022 “ a tremendous success with great participation & breathtaking engagement through the day on 17th Nov 2022 at SAP Labs India Bengaluru.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/11/keynote-speakers.jpg)

**Shri Raksha** emceed the conference with her fervour enthusiasm and the day kick-started with the welcome message from **Sindhu Gangadharan**, SVP & MD SAP Labs India & Head, SAP User Enablement, this was followed by keynote from **Steffen Burger**, SVP, SAP Partner Solution Adoption, Partner Ecosystem Success SAP Deutschland SE & Co. KG detailing on the “SAP Partner Strategy” followed by **Marc Thier**, SVP Application Lifecycle Management SAP SE explaining the “The Future of ALM – Partner Perspective”. This evidently simplified the futuristic path for all the partners in the ALM Space and positioning of SAP ALM tools to our customers.

**Marc** **Thier** also explained the direction for partners and the white spaces in SAP Cloud ALM  which partners can explore and as well on the technologies that they should get proficient on the tools like SAP Signavio, Tricentis Test Automation for SAP (acronym: TTA) and SAP BTP extensions etc,.

**Michel Kloeffer**, Chief Product Owner Customer Experience & Solutions SAP SE   explained the implementation capability, roadmap and how to help customers in the Digital Transformation Journey using SAP Cloud ALM.

**Janko Budzisch,** Chief Product Owner Customer Experience & Solutions SAP SE took the audience on how to “Efficiently manage your customers intelligent sustainable enterprise “with SAP Cloud ALM.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/11/fire-side-chat.png)

Fire-side chat hosted by **Sonal Kumar** Head of SAP ALM Go-to-market APJ SAP Labs India with above keynote speakers and  **Sharath Shasheendran** Director, Regional Lead ALM, India, Partners APJ, SAP Labs India

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/11/Pic-3.jpg)

We also had partners from SAT Infotech Private Ltd, Ivalue, Blueprint Technologies Pvt Ltd, HCL Technologies, KTern.AI, who shared Implementation and the Operations experience in the SAP Cloud ALM Space.

**Conclusion & Call For Action -**

SAP Solution Manager follows the maintenance strategy of the SAP Business Suite 2027) extended maintenance with Business suite 2030 which supports  process management, test suite, change & release management and ITSM in SAP Solution Manager 7.2 is automatically included in the optional extended maintenance for the SAP Business Suite 7.​

SAP Cloud ALM and SAP Focused Run are not dependent on the SAP Business Suite, allowing mainstream maintenance beyond 2030. SAP plans no new on premise products in the ALM portfolio.

SAP Cloud is free and Usage rights are included in the SAP Cloud Service subscription and in SAP Enterprise Support.
