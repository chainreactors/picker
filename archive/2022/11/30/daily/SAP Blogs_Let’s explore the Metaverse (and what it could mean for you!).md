Let's move the fog away, eliminate the white noise and look into what value and opportunities the Metaverse can bring to you.

Why? Because I think that there's more than the buzz and that the Metaverse [is not dead](https://venturebeat-com.cdn.ampproject.org/c/s/venturebeat.com/virtual/no-the-metaverse-is-not-dead-its-inevitable/amp/).

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/11/292172_GettyImages-507094667_small_jpg.jpg)

If you however accept the idea that the Metaverse is inevitable, the question is: How to handle it?

My colleagues of the SAP Intelligent Enterprise Institute are developing a structured workshop  format to explore in 1 day (virtual or on-site) what you could do to go forward.

The workshop starts with Inspiration of early Metaverse scenarios and relevant business fields, followed by a guided exploration to prioritize your metaverse use cases and to link the business and technology view on those.

The result of the workshop is a detailed executive summary with top Metaverse use cases and way
forward - and at the same time, you bring your team together and create a shared understanding on Metaverse opportunities.

While we are developing this format, we are looking for companies - SAP customer or not! - to join us with their teams for a the first proof-of-concept workshops.

If you are interested, please contact me!
