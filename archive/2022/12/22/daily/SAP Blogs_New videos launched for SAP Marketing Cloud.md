Hi Folks,

Greetings of the festive season!

We have an exciting lineup of videos for you from SAP Marketing Cloud:

|
 [Health Checks and Best Practices for SAP Marketing Cloud](https://microlearning.opensap.com/media/1_2tlcaiq2) |
 This video will show you the health check feature for campaigns, which are carried out on the Check Your Marketing Solution app |

|
 [Optimizing the Scheduling of Your Campaigns Using Campaign Analytics Report](https://microlearning.opensap.com/media/1_sn8sfivh/) |
 This video will introduce two new operational reports available in SAP Marketing Cloud, which are the Campaign Action Execution Schedule and the Campaign Action Schedule by Target Group. |

|
 [Introduction to Change Log for Campaigns](https://microlearning.opensap.com/media/1_p89ig2hf/) |
 This video will introduce the new feature of Change Log tab in the Campaigns app. |

Check it out now!!
