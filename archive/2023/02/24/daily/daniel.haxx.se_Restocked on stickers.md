# Restocked on stickers

[February 23, 2023](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2023/02/23/restocked-on-stickers/) [Daniel Stenberg](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/author/daniel/) [2 Comments](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2023/02/23/restocked-on-stickers/#comments)

I attended FOSDEM 2023 and over the two days of conference I gave away well over a thousand curl stickers. Every last curl sticker I had in my possession.

What does a curl maintainer do when he runs out of curl stickers? He restocks.

[![](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/box-of-stickers.jpg)](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/box-of-stickers.jpg)

5400 stickes and 200 coasters arrived in a box.

[![](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/many-tickers.jpg)](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/many-tickers.jpg)

Many stickers.

[![](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/new-sticker-designs.jpg)](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/new-sticker-designs.jpg)

Two new sticker designs in a limited edition.

## Getting stickers

Right now, the only way to get your hands on a set of these fine quality stickers is to meet me. Attend a talk that I do or a conference that I visit.

I might do another attempt at distributing stickers at a later point, but [the last time](https://daniel.haxx.se/blog/2021/05/28/giving-away-an-insane-amount-of-curl-stickers/) did leave some scars I still haven‚Äôt gotten over!
