Hello SAP Community,

# \*Updated Date: 4th March 2023\*

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/12/SITBLR23.png)

We are excited to announce that speaker registration is now open for the [SAP Inside Track Bengaluru 03/2023](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/11/08/announcing-sap-inside-track-in-bengaluru-2023-sitblr2023/) on **4th March 2023 – Updated from**~~**25th Feb 2023**~~ , taking place in Bengaluru. We are looking for speakers to share their insights, knowledge and experiences with the SAP Community.

If you are an expert in any SAP technologies or want to share your knowledge with the community, we encourage you to apply as a speaker. In addition to the opportunity to showcase your work, speaking at the event can provide valuable networking opportunities and exposure for your business, organization or yourself.

## Topics

The only criterion is that the topic should be related to SAP. There are also exceptions for non-SAP topics, e.g., Writing a Book, related to SAP Community etc.,

Some of the areas you can present on but not limited to: SAP Application Development, Low Code No Code, SAP Functional Areas, Integration, Security, Cloud, Strategy & Design.

Any new ideas are also most welcome ![:slightly_smiling_face:](/html/@B7DA5BDB859A29208BCFC4278E960B2C/emoticons/1f642.png ":slightly_smiling_face:")

## Speaker Registration

To apply, please fill out the speaker registration form below.

[Link for Speaker registration](https://forms.gle/RZaqKp413mUW9TXN6)

**Note:** This is an In-person Event and Speaker is expected to be in Bengaluru, SAP Labs India.

## Closing

If you have any questions, please get in touch with us at sapcommunitybengaluru@gmail.com. We look forward to reviewing your session submission and featuring your expertise at the event.

Please help spread the word about speaker registration by **sharing this post** with your network.

Thank you,

Syam
