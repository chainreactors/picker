Dear reader,

We are very pleased to invite you to the second virtual localization event for Latin America. It is aimed at the SAP community in the region.

This event will feature several virtual sessions in which you will be able to obtain an overview of SAP’s localized solutions for Latin America and the innovations on them.

Today it is very important for your organization to be able to run your business both locally and globally, so the information that the experts will share in this opportunity will be very useful.

Among the main topics, information will be shared on the localization of SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition for the region, its new local versions released this year and the short-term roadmap. The presentation of SAP Document and Reporting Compliance will be added, the ideal complement for your ERP to process and exchange legal documentation with its business partners and the government.

Another key point of your company’s capital is human resources, therefore, experts from SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central, SAP SuccessFactors Central Payroll & SAP HCM for S/4HANA from Latin America will present the localization news for these solutions in the region.

Following, the quality processes that are part of the SAP development cycle will be presented, as well as SAP support channels and processes.

You will also have the opportunity to meet localization product managers from the region, who will share the latest and best documentation of local versions for their country. Adding to this, the RISE with SAP and SAP SERVICES teams, will provide information about their services, which will be very useful for your company, optimizing both new implementations and existing processes as well.

The virtual seminar will be held mainly in Spanish, with a session in English dedicated to the e-Social digital payroll solution – HXM Brazil.
