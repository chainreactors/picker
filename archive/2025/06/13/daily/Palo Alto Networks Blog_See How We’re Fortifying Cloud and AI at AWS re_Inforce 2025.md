Get ready for AWS re:Inforce 2025, the only learning conference focused exclusively on AWS security solutions, cloud security, compliance and identity. Palo Alto Networks will be there in force, giving attendees the opportunity to learn about overcoming the latest security challenges through highly-focused breakout sessions, hands-on AI security workshops, and in-booth demonstrations designed to show ways you can secure your data, workloads, applications and environments. Join us in Philadelphia from June 16-18 to connect with AWS security experts and discover actionable solutions to your organization's security challenges.

## What to Expect from Palo Alto Networks at AWS re:Inforce 2025

We're bringing a comprehensive experience to [re:Inforce 2025](https://reinforce.awsevents.com/), designed to provide valuable insights and practical solutions for practitioners and decision-makers alike. We invite you to visit booth #1317 at the event to engage with our experts and witness our solutions in action. We are also hosting two 60-minute breakout sessions, offering deep dives into critical security topics, designed to help secure your AWS journey. You can also enhance your learning with our specialized hands-on JAM Workshop focused on securing your AI environment. For dedicated 1:1 discussions, a private meeting room will be available.

### **Featured Speaking Sessions**

Join us for our sponsored breakout sessions to gain deeper insights into critical cloud security topics:

**Building Stronger Cloud Defense with Real-World Insights**

Discuss real-world cloud threats and reveal patterns and intelligence for successful incident response and robust detection and remediation capabilities. Attendees will learn how Unit 42, in partnership with AWS, are helping organizations navigate the cloud security landscape.

* + **When:** Tuesday, June 17, at 1:30 PM - 2:30PM
  + **Location:** 200 Level, Hall D, Blue Theater

**JAM Workshop – Prisma AIRS – Securing AI**

This hands-on workshop will help you sharpen your AWS security skills in a gamified learning experience. Collaborate with a team to navigate simulated real-world scenarios. Engage with leading subject matter experts, get your questions answered, and deepen your security knowledge through guided exercises.

* + **When:** Tuesday, June 17, at 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  + **Location:** 100 Level, Room 122B

**Protecting GenAI and the Entire AI Ecosystem**

Explore how the evolving threat landscape impacts AI applications in AWS, and discover how Palo Alto Networks helps manage risks throughout the AI ecosystem. We will showcase solutions for granular access control to third-party AI services (like ChatGPT and DeepSeek), as well as protection of your entire AI stack – covering data, supply chain, applications and runtime – ensuring your AI/ML environments are secure, compliant and resilient to attacks. Additionally, it will demonstrate how the Palo Alto Networks platform, powered by Precision AI, prevents polymorphic threats.

* + **When:** Wednesday, June 18, at 11:30 AM -12:30 PM
  + **Location:** 100 Level, Room 118B

### **Dive Deep into AWS Cloud Security with Our In-Booth Demos**

When you visit our booth you’ll discover multiple demo stations showcasing our cutting-edge solutions. Here’s a sneak peek at what you’ll learn:

* **Securing Bedrock and AI Models by Design with Prisma AIRS –** Learn how [Prisma AIRS](/prisma/prisma-ai-runtime-security) discovers, assesses and protects enterprise AI systems against both AI-specific and fundamental network threats.
* **Cloud NGFW for AWS: Fully Managed. AI-Powered. Instantly Scalable –** Learn how [Cloud NGFW](/network-security/cloud-ngfw), our cloud-native AWS next-gen firewall, simplifies managing your AWS network security without modifying your infrastructure, all in an AWS PAYG consumption model.
* **Securing AWS Applications with Palo Alto Networks Software Firewalls –** Explore how our [VM-Series Software Firewall](/network-security/software-firewalls) adds deep visibility and real time protection to your evolving AWS environments, stopping malware, zero-days and network intrusions with advanced AI-powered threat prevention.
* **AI-Powered SASE for Your Next Wave of Digital Transformation –** See how [Prisma SASE](/sase) secures every user, device and application with AI-powered zero trust security, blocking GenAI-based threats and delivering peak performance.
* **Building Securely in AWS with AI-Driven Application Security (AppSec) –** Learn about prevention-first [AppSec](/cortex/cloud/application-security) that intelligently stops risk from being introduced, saving time by blocking up to 90% of risks from reaching production.
* **Securing Your AWS Environments From Code to Cloud to SOC –** Discover how [Cortex Cloud](/cortex/cloud) merges best-in-class CDR with industry-leading CNAPP for real-time cloud security, leveraging AI and automation to prioritize risks and stop attacks faster.
* **Transforming End-to-End Security Operations with AI and Automation –** Witness an [AI-driven SOC](/cortex/security-operations-automation) unify security operations across code, cloud, endpoints and networks, drastically reducing mean time to detect and respond to threats.
* **Expert Insight from Unit 42 on Defeating the Latest Cloud Attacks –** Gain valuable knowledge on swift and effective cloud incident response, comprehensive cloud security strategy and ongoing cloud defense through [Unit 42](/cortex/managed-detection-and-response) offerings.
