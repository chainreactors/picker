* Are you struggling to be able to predict product demand?

* Are you anxious because of inaccurate product demand forecast results?

* Are you always worry about the capacity utilization situation?

If you have answered “Yes” to any of these questions, we invite you to join the sessions organized by **SAP Intelligent Adoption and Consumption Team** in collaboration with Product Management, on Predictive Material and Resource Planning in SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition.

Predictive Material and Resource Planning (pMRP) is relevant for a broad range of industries and applied to different business scenarios. The SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition offers an end-to-end process to help you realize Predictive MRP.
