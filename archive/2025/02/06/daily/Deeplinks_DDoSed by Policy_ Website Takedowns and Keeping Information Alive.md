![Person in suit censoring words on a page](https://www.eff.org/files/styles/resized_banner/public/banner_library/icaan-censor-1.jpg?itok=3Z7F20Nd "ICAAN Censor")

Who needs a DDoS (Denial of Service) attack when you have a new president? As of [February 2nd](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/02/02/upshot/trump-government-websites-missing-pages.html?unlocked_article_code=1.uE4.sU_u.kLkd45LyHPc6&smid=nytcore-ios-share&referringSource=articleShare), thousands of web pages and datasets have been removed from U.S. government agencies following a series of executive orders. The impacts span the [Department of Veteran Affairs](https://www.va.gov/) and the [Center of Disease Control and Prevention](https://www.cnn.com/2025/01/31/health/cdc-websites-gender-lgbtq-datasets/index.html), all the way to programs like [Head Start](https://headstart.gov/).

Government workers had just two days to carry out [sweeping takedowns and rewrites](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/01/31/health/trump-cdc-dei-gender.html) due to a memo from the [Office of Personnel Management](https://chcoc.gov/content/initial-guidance-regarding-president-trump%E2%80%99s-executive-order-defending-women). The memo cites a [recent executive order](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/defending-women-from-gender-ideology-extremism-and-restoring-biological-truth-to-the-federal-government/) [attacking](https://www.pbs.org/newshour/politics/6-ways-trumps-executive-orders-are-targeting-transgender-people) Trans people and further stigmatizing them...
