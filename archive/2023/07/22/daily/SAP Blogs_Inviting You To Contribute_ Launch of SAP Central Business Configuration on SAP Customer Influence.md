We're thrilled to announce that we've launched a dedicated site for SAP Central Business Configuration on the [Customer Influence platform](https://www.sap.com/about/customer-involvement/influence-adopt.html)\* – your go-to channel to influence SAP cloud solutions by suggesting improvements. We’d like to extend a warm welcome to everyone and invite you to join.

The dedicated [SAP Central Business Configuration site](https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/campaign/3507)\* on Customer Influence is more than just a platform; it's a community. We've designed it with the aim of fostering collaboration and innovation. It's a space where we can gather your invaluable insights and ideas to improve our software and develop features that we haven't offered yet, but could greatly benefit your everyday work.

We want to encourage each one of you to actively participate: Share your ideas by proposing new features or suggesting improvements of existing ones and vote for the proposals made by others that you find useful. Even though not every suggestion will end up being developed, every idea counts, and those that reach 10 or more votes will be reviewed by our team. It's a perfect opportunity to directly influence the future development of SAP Central Business Configuration.

Please be aware that our site is specifically for SAP Central Business Configuration for SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition. Topics related to Manage Your Solution and Configure Your Solution, the SAP solution builder tool, as well as SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition and SAP S/4HANA, will not be covered.

We’re delighted to introduce this new form of interaction with you. It's a significant step towards improving and evolving SAP Central Business Configuration hand-in-hand with our users. We're excited to embark on this journey of continuous, joint improvement, and we're eager to see all the fantastic ideas that this collaboration will bring to the table.

Let's make SAP Central Business Configuration even better, together!

\*Please note that an S-user is required to access Customer Influence.
