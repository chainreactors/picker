# Sandfly Now Available for Free at Digital Ocean

10 June 2021

Product Update

The Digital Ocean App Marketplace now has images of Sandfly available:

[Digital Ocean Sandfly Marketplace Image](https://marketplace.digitalocean.com/apps/sandflysecurity)

Digital Ocean users can now setup Sandfly with 1-Click install and begin scanning your hosts for compromise and suspicious activity. There is a free built-in five host license to get you going immediately. If you need more licenses, please [contact us](/contact-us/).
