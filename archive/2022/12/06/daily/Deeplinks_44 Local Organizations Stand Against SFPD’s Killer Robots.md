EFF is announcing a letter signed by 44 community groups who stand united in opposition to the San Francisco Board of Supervisors authorizing the San Francisco Police Department to deploy deadly force with remote-control robots. The signers include racial justice groups, civil rights and civil liberties organizations, LGBTQ organizations, and labor unions.

You can read the entire letter [here](https://www.eff.org/document/san-francisco-killer-robot-local-coalition-letter).

From the letter:

> “SFPD’s proposal, if approved, threatens the privacy and safety of city residents and visitors. Black Americans are [three times more likely](https://www.hsph.harvard.edu/news/hsph-in-the-news/blacks-whites-police-deaths-disparity/) than white Americans to be killed during police encounters.
