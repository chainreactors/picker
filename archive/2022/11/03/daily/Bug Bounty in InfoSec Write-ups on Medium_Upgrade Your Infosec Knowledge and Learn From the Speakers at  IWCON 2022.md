Hello hackers!

We at [**Infosec Writeups**](https://infosecwriteups.com/) are organizing[**IWCon 2022**](https://iwcon.live/) — the second edition of our international cybersecurity conference and networking event on 17–18 December 2022.

The conference is open for cybersecurity researchers from all over the world, and [**the entry ticket is just $10**](https://razorpay.com/payment-button/pl_K8cxPtmUyBH2PC/view).

We have some amazing speakers sharing their personal stories and unique experiences of how they established themselves in Infosec.

Be a part of this cool bunch, and book your spot [**here**](https://razorpay.com/payment-button/pl_K8cxPtmUyBH2PC/view).
