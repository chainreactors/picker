The SAP Commerce Cloud product team are inviting customers to share their feedback and ideas to enhance our solution. Through SAP Continuous Influence you can submit your improvement requests, vote on other ideas posted by peers and get regular notifications about the statuses of requests.

The product team regularly evaluates the ideas and considers them for roadmap planning. Votes help to prioritise ideas along with other important selection criteria such as:

* **DESIRABILITY**: How many customers voted for this? How many customers will benefit from it?

* **VIABILITY**: Is this Improvement Request globally relevant? Is this in alignment with SAP’s strategy for the product?

* **FEASIBILITY**: Is the development effort realistic? Is this request achievable within the product’s architecture?

Continuous Influence for SAP Commerce Cloud is accessible to all SAP Commerce Cloud customers. **Follow the steps below to get access** and start sharing your enhancement ideas:

* **Go to** [SAP Customer Influence](https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/) home page

  + Login with your S-User and accept the Terms of Use

* **Navigate** to the list of [SAP Commerce Cloud Continuous Influence Sessions](https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/campaigns-open/?sort=SUBMIT_TO%2520ASC&tags=%255B%257B%2522ID%2522:2,%2522NAME%2522:%2522Commerce%2522,%2522GROUP_ID%2522:8,%2522ROOTGROUPID%2522:3%257D,%257B%2522ID%2522:60,%2522NAME%2522:%2522SAP%252520Continuous%252520Influence%2522,%2522GROUP_ID%2522:5,%2522ROOTGROUPID%2522:5%257D%255D) and **register** to be able to submit improvement requests**.**

  + Approval of registration is typically done in <1 business day

* **Follow**the session to get notified of new Improvement Requests and blogs

* **Search, v****ote** and **comment** on Improvement Requests posted by peer customers

* **Submit** new Improvement Requests
