# Uniting the brightest minds in security, network and cloud

## Immerse is Cloudflare’s premier annual conference in Southeast Asia

[Contributed by Cloudflare](/Author/Contributed-by-Cloudflare "Read more by this author")

Fri 23 Aug 2024  //
03:26 UTC

![](/design_picker/d2e337b97204af4aa34dda04c4e5d56d954b216f/graphics/icons/social_share_icon.svg)

Partner Content Cloudflare is excited to present Immerse, our flagship event designed to connect attendees directly with the ideas, technologies and business leaders driving network and security transformation.

Building on the momentum of our Connect event on August 6, Immerse will tour across three major cities in Southeast Asia – [Singapore](https://www.reg-events.com/cloudflare-immerse-singapore-2024?utm_source=the-register&u%5b%E2%80%A6%5d_campaign=cloudflare-immerse-series-2024&utm_content=banner) on the 15 August, [Bangkok](https://www.reg-events.com/cloudflare-immerse-bangkok-2024?utm_source=the-register&utm%5b%E2%80%A6%5d_campaign=cloudflare-immerse-series-2024&utm_content=banner) on the 12 September and [Kuala Lumpur](https://www.reg-events.com/cloudflare-immerse-kualalumpur-2024?utm_source=the-register%5b%E2%80%A6%5d_campaign=cloudflare-immerse-series-2024&utm_content=banner) on the 25 September.

This year's tour is dedicated to enabling organizations to leverage connectivity cloud solutions to regain control while connecting and protecting people, apps, and data anywhere amidst the evolving threat landscape. To achieve this, we are bringing together the brightest minds in security, network and cloud practitioners, along with our technology partners, end users, and local partners in a series of local chapters.
