[**User Experience in Sales Apps for SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition**](https://url.sap/5qsj3n)

SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition has changed its appearance over last few releases. Its interface has become much easier to navigate and users are finding it easer to access business insights, tasks, and applications.

This is result SAPs commitment to improve speed and agility of S/4HANA Cloud, public edition while striving to improve user experience.
