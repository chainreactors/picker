# Robinsons Malls

## What Happened

In June 2024, the Philippines' largest shopping-mall operators [Robinsons Malls suffered a data breach stemming from their mobile app](https://www.rappler.com/technology/national-privacy-commission-confirms-data-breach-toyota-robinson-sr-quiet-hack/). The incident exposed 195k unique email addresses along with names, phone numbers, dates of birth, genders and the user's city and province.

## Compromised Data

Dates of birth

Email addresses

Genders

Geographic locations

Names

Phone numbers
