We have completed the Q4/2022 review round for SAP Continuous Influence for **SAP S/4HANA Cloud, Public Edition.** Find out about the deliveries and progress made in each individual session blog post below.

The posts will outline which request:

+ have been delivered or are already available

+ have been moved forward in the portfolio planning process (accepted / planned long-term)

+ have not (yet) made it into them product portfolio, since further evaluation may be required (for long-term consideration),

+ were not accepted for planning

Thank you for your continuous feedback throughout 2022 helping us to improve our public cloud offering with and for you.
