## Keychain Access app in new location on macOS Sequoia

September 16, 2024
[rtrouton](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/author/rtrouton/ "Posts by rtrouton") [Leave a comment](#respond)
[Go to comments](#comments)

One of the changes made as part of macOS Sequoia is that the location of [Keychain Access.app](https://support.apple.com/guide/keychain-access/what-is-keychain-access-kyca1083/mac) has moved:

In macOS Sonoma and earlier, **Keychain Access.app** is in the following location:

**/Applications/Utilities/Keychain Access.app**

![](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/screenshot-2024-09-16-at-4.14.55e280afpm.png?w=599&h=302 "Screenshot 2024-09-16 at 4.14.55 PM.png")

As of macOS Sequoia, **Keychain Access.app** is in the following location:

**/System/Library/CoreServices/Applications/Keychain Access.app**

**![](https://derflounder.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/screenshot-2024-09-16-at-4.17.48e280afpm.png?w=600&h=148 "Screenshot 2024-09-16 at 4.17.48 PM.png")**
