CertWatcher is a tool for capture and tracking [certificate transparency](https://www.kitploit.com/search/label/Certificate%20Transparency "certificate transparency") logs, using YAML templates. The tool helps to detect and analyze [phishing](https://www.kitploit.com/search/label/Phishing "phishing") websites and [regular expression](https://www.kitploit.com/search/label/Regular%20Expression "regular expression") patterns, and is designed to make it [easy to use](https://www.kitploit.com/search/label/Easy%20To%20Use "easy to use") for security professionals and researchers.

Certwatcher continuously monitors the certificate data stream and checks for suspicious patterns or malicious activity. Certwatcher can also be customized to detect specific phishing patterns and combat the spread of malicious websites.

## Get Started

Certwatcher allows you to use custom templates to display the certificate information. We have some public custom templates available from the community. You can find them in our [repository](https://github.com/drfabiocastro/certwatcher-templates "repository").
