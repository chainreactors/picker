## Proactive strategies for data security and identity management in 2025

[Annaliese Ingrams](/Author/Annaliese-Ingrams "Read more by this author")

Thu 16 Jan 2025  //
16:11 UTC

![](/design_picker/d2e337b97204af4aa34dda04c4e5d56d954b216f/graphics/icons/social_share_icon.svg)

Webinar Are you tired of constant firefighting in the ever-changing cybersecurity landscape?

It may be time to break the cycle and take control with a proactive approach to data security and identity management. Find out how by joining industry experts Vileen Dhutia from Rubrik and Tim Phillips from The Register in an exclusive webinar on [23rd January 2025 at 3am PST/6am EDT/11am GMT](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/34367/proactive-data-security-and-identity-management), where they'll share practical strategies to safeguard your organisation's data for 2025 and beyond.

In this session, you'll discover how you could evolve from traditional perimeter, endpoint, and application-centric approaches to a more forward-thinking, holistic security strategy. You'll also get tips on how modern tools and practices can help you not only protect your data but also drive lasting organisational change.

Key takeaways include:

*- Turning reactive habits into proactive security measures.*

*- Adopting advanced tools and strategies to stay ahead of threats.*

*- Building a resilient organisational culture around data security.*

Take this opportunity to break free from the reactive cycle. Sign up to access this [webinar](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/34367/proactive-data-security-and-identity-management) now to help ensure your data is protected in 2025 and beyond.
