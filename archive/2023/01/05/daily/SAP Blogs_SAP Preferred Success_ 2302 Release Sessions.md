**√   Do you want to know what is part of the release?
√   Do you test your processes automatically?
√   Did you want to understand changes in roles and authorizations?**

If you have answered "Yes" to any of these questions, we invite you to join sessions on **“2302 Release Planning, Access Management & Automated Testing sessions for****SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition”**

Hope you have enjoyed the sessions. If you wish you can view the recordings by clicking on book. **We invite you to influence the adoption events by completing the survey [here](https://basecamphcotmv577w.fra1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_9uVmGJHmvhMJGhU?rc=1).**

Learn more about SAP Preferred Success [here](https://www.sap.com/services-support/support-plans/advanced-support.html).

We look forward to helping you in your SAP S/4HANA Cloud journey. Feel free to write us at s4hana\_cloud\_adoption@sap.com
