With the final negotiating session to approve the UN Cybercrime Treaty just days away, EFF and 21 international civil society organizations today [urgently called on delegates](https://www.eff.org/document/urgent-appealuntreaty) from EU states and the European Commission to push back on the draft convention's many flaws, which include an excessively broad scope that will grant intrusive surveillance powers without robust human rights and data protection safeguards.
