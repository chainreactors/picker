Young people have a right to speak and access information online. Legislatures should remember that protecting kids' online safety shouldn't require sweeping online surveillance and censorship.

EFF reminded the New York Attorney General of this important fact in comments responding to the state's recently passed [Stop Addictive Feeds Exploitation](https://www.nysenate.gov/legislation/bills/2023/S7694/amendment/A) (SAFE) for Kids Act—which requires platforms to verify the ages of people who visit them. After New York's legislature passed the bill, it is now up to the state attorney general's office to write rules to implement it.
