# BforeAI Secures Series B Funding to Preempt Malicious Attacks Through PreCrime™ AI

Funded by Titanium Ventures, investment will accelerate growth into new markets.

**New York, NY – January 14, 2025 –** BforeAI, the world’s fastest and most accurate predictive attack intelligence and digital risk protection solution, announced today the company has closed an oversubscribed $10 million Series B round of funding led by Titanium Ventures. This investment round brings the company’s total funding to over $30 million and will accelerate market expansion in the utilities, pharmaceutical and healthcare sectors as the company continues to enhance its PreCrime™ platform, expand sales resources, and strengthen partnerships.
