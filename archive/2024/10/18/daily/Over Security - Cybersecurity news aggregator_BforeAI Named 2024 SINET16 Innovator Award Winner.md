## BforeAI Named 2024 SINET16 Innovator Award Winner

![2024 sinet16 innovator award](https://bfore.ai/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Blog-Images-1920-×-1200-px-5-e1752157704171-1024x799.png)

**New York, NY – October 17, 2024** – BforeAI, the world’s fastest and most accurate predictive attack intelligence and digital risk protection solution, announced today that it has been named a 2024 SINET16 Innovator Award winner.

SINET is dedicated to introducing leading innovators into the cybersecurity industry and accelerating innovation by encouraging collaboration, breaking down communication barriers, and facilitating high-level sharing of ideas and best practices between senior-level security professionals from the private and public sectors and solution providers, buyers, and investors. For the SINET16 Innovator Awards, winners were selected from a pool of 230 applications from 13 countries this year, all companies with under $15 million in revenue. The 16 emerging companies, including BforeAI, are identified as the most innovative and compelling technologies in their fields to address cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities.
