## Quad9 and BforeAI Expand Strategic Partnership to Supercharge Preemptive Threat Intelligence

![strategic partnership with quad9 and bforeai](https://bfore.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/strategic-partnership-with-quad9-and-bforeai.png)

**Zurich, Switzerland and New York, New York — 11 June 2025**

In an important step forward for global cybersecurity, Quad9 and BforeAI are expanding their strategic partnership to accelerate protection against cyber attacks before they occur. As a newly announced Quad9 sponsor, BforeAI is deepening its commitment to Quad9’s mission of providing privacy-respecting, secure DNS services for millions of users worldwide.

In this enhanced collaboration, BforeAI continues to integrate its PreCrime™ Intelligence feeds into the Quad9 threat blocking platform. PreCrime Intelligence provides 98% unique predictive threat indicators compared to conventional platforms, identifying malicious infrastructure in its infancy and enabling Quad9 to preemptively block user access to fraudulent and harmful domains.
