# Sandfly Secures Vay's Remotely Driven Vehicle Fleet

22 July 2025

Case Study

Operating vehicles remotely requires the utmost in safety and reliability. That's why [Vay](https://vay.io/), the leader in remote driving technology, selected Sandfly to protect their vehicle fleet.

In this case study you can learn more about Vay's unique challenges in vehicle security and how Sandfly was able to help them ensure safety and reliability where it matters most. Without deploying endpoint agents, Sandfly's agentless approach gave Vay the confidence to ensure their vehicles were secure without interfering with critical safety systems needed to operate.

Read the case study below to find out more:
