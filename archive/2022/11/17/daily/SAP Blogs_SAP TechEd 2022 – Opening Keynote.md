## *Day 1 Highlights*

SAP focused on key areas to improve its core platform and offers an end-to-end business process for enterprises for various industry-specific solutions. Some key areas where SAP invests a lot to improve overall enterprise applications.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/11/1-73.png)

SAP Business Technology Platform

Extend and personalize SAP applications or integrate and connect entire landscapes with SAP Business Technology Platform. Unleash your business users to connect processes and experiences, decide confidently with integrity and continuous business innovation.

SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP) brings together data and analytics, artificial intelligence, application development, automation, and integration in one, unified environment.

A platform built with flexibility and choice in mind can help you create applications that simply work, all without a single line of code.

**15000+ customers are already live with BTP.**

## *Integration Suite*

SAP Integration Suite is an integration platform as a service (iPaaS) that allows you to smoothly integrate on-premise and cloud-based applications and processes with tools and prebuilt content managed by SAP.

Cloud Integration

* Prebuilt integrations managed and updated by SAP

* Harmonized access to popular third-party cloud applications

* Ability to design, publish, and manage APIs

* AI-enabled cloud and hybrid integration development

* Guided systematic approach to define, document, and govern your integration strategy
