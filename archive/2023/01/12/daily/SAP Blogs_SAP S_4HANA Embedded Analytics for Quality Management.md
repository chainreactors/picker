# Quality Management in digital Transformation

Digital transformation brings fundamental changes to the way work will be done in the future. The impacts can also be felt in the field of Quality Management. Changing customer needs and increased expectations for quality require an adjustment of the role of quality management. Today, it is no longer limited to just ensuring product and service quality, but also plays an important role in business development. This change offers the opportunity to reorient quality management in a future-oriented way. The goal should be to develop an intelligent approach that goes beyond compliance with regulations and cost savings and creates additional value - the approach of «smart Quality Management».

# The Importance of Quality Data in the Future

An important aspect of a smart Quality Management is the use of quality data, which allows for the collection, analysis, and derivation of action recommendations of large amounts of quality data. In the field of quality management, this development has particular relevance, as data can contribute to improving the quality of products and processes while reducing costs. Smart quality management is also defined by the generation of additional value for a company such as improving decision-making through real-time information."

# The way to «smart Quality Management»

One way to use data to our advantage is by using SAP S/4HANA Embedded Analytics.

SAP S/4HANA Embedded Analytics is an important technology that allows companies to integrate real-time business data into their applications and processes. This can contribute to companies making faster and better decisions by providing their employees with access to relevant and current data.

SAP S/4HANA Embedded Analytics also offers advanced analytics features, such as the ability to create and share dashboards and reports. This helps companies better visualize and understand their data and allows them to make informed decisions based on real-time data.

In summary, it can be said that SAP S/4HANA Embedded Analytics is an essential technology for companies that want to optimize their quality data and take an important step towards «smart Quality Management.

# Why Quality Managers should use embedded analytics

In today's business world, it is important to ensure the quality of products and services. Quality management has become an important part of corporate strategy, as it helps to strengthen customer loyalty and reduce the risk of errors and recalls. An effective method of improving quality management is to use SAP S/4HANA Embedded Analytics. This enables quality managers to collect, analyze, and visualize relevant data in real-time, identify and solve problems faster, and avoid future sources of errors. Additionally, it allows quality management to access relevant information without accessing separate tools or applications, thus enabling faster and more effective collaboration. With dashboards and reports, quality management can monitor the quality of products and services throughout the entire value chain, improve processes, and accelerate decisions. SAP S/4HANA Embedded Analytics also allows one to navigate directly in business applications (Insight-to-Action approach) and provide users with personalized views.
