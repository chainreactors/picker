## Fusion Fireside #5: The Art of Fusion with Jake Norwood

07 November 2024

![](https://www.threatfabric.com/hubfs/TF_FusionFireside_JakeNorwood.jpg)

### Jump to

This episode features Jake Norwood, a leader in the field of cybersecurity and threat intelligence. Hosted by Eward Driehuis, this episode offers insights into Jake’s rich experience, Threat Intelligence, and Fusion.

##

## Meet Jake Norwood

Jake Norwood is a seasoned cybersecurity executive currently leading Advanced Cyber Defense solutions in Europe and the UK for Booz Allen Hamilton. With a rich background that includes serving as the Global Head of Citigroup’s Cyber Fusion Centers and directing Cyber Intelligence at Citi, Jake brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to the table.

## In this Episode

In this episode, Jake shares insights on the following topics:

* **From Battlefield to Boardroom:** Jake’s transition from military intelligence to the finance sector. What challenges did he face, and what war stories does he have to share?
* **European Adventures:** Why did Jake move to Europe, and how is he finding life and work across the pond?
* **Building Fusion Centers:** When did Jake first start building fusion centers, and what has his involvement been like over the years?
* **Defining Fusion:** How does Jake define the concept of Fusion, and what are the key ingredients for a successful fusion center?
* **US vs. Europe:** Are there differences in how Europe handles cybersecurity compared to the US?
* **Trends in Financial Institutions:** What trends is Jake seeing in financial institutions (FIs) today?
* **MTI and Fraud Prevention:** What are the latest trends in Managed Threat Intelligence (MTI) and fraud prevention?
* **The Role of Law and Regulation:** How are laws and regulations shaping the cybersecurity landscape?
* **Looking Ahead:** What does the future hold for Jake and the field of cybersecurity?

Join us for this exciting episode of Fusion Fireside, where we delve deep into the world of online fraud and threat intelligence with one of the industry’s leading experts.
