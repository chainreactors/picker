After the successful [SAP Insider event](https://blogs.sap.com/2023/06/13/recap-of-sap-sit-event-milwaukee-usa/) in Milwaukee, we are ready for the next event at Brookfield, Wisconsin.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/06/Milwaukee2.jpg)

**What is SAP Inside Track?**

“An SAP Inside Track is a local grassroots community event where SAP Community members come together to share knowledge about SAP-related topics.” This is an informal event organized by SAP Community members; anyone interested can participate **free of charge**.

NOTE: SAP Insider Track is an SAP Community event organized by SAP Community members.

**What is in it for you?**

1. **Networking** – You can connect with SAP Carrer Councilors, Developers, Consultants, Architects, Mentors & Champions.

2. **Learning** – You learn about  SAP IT career path planning along with the latest SAP technologies from the experts working for customers, partners & SAP.

3. **Recognition** – You will be recognized in SAP Community & other social networks(LinkedIn & Twitter) for your technical skills in presenting on various tech topics.

4. **Presentation, Communication & Leadership skills** – If you are presenting a session or volunteering in SAP Inside Track, this will help you tone your presentation, communication & leadership skills.
