The recently concluded 2-day Intelligent Enterprise Journey Workshop powered by SAP Signavio Process Insight for **IBM** was a huge success. The online workshop, which took place on February 02 and 03 and lasted 2 hours each day, was attended by 25 **IBM** employees. The workshop aimed to support partners who are positioning and delivering RISE with SAP by providing them with the necessary tools to leverage SAP Signavio Process Insights.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/02/IBM-–-Intelligent-Enterprise-Journey-Workshop-powered-by-SAP-Signavio-Process-Insights-small.png)

IBM – Intelligent Enterprise Journey Workshop powered by SAP Signavio Process Insights

[SAP Signavio Process Insights](https://www.signavio.com/products/process-insights/) is a process analytics solution that helps businesses quickly identify areas for improvement and automation within their SAP business processes. The workshop provided attendees with a mix of presentations and hands-on exercises using MURAL, allowing them to learn how to run workshops and work out an innovation roadmap with a customer.

The session started with moty.moshin.sap welcoming everyone and introducing the 2 day's agenda.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/02/IBM-–-Intelligent-Enterprise-Journey-Workshop-powered-by-SAP-Signavio-Process-Insights-Agenda-1.png)

IBM – Intelligent Enterprise Journey Workshop powered by SAP Signavio Process Insights - Agenda

And volker.anders from the SAP Signavio Center of Excellence followed, giving an Up-To-Date overview of SAP Signavio Process Transformation Suite.

moty.moshin.sap continued by providing deep dive into SAP Signavio Process Insights and a demo right after that; moty.moshin.sap provided the Case Study introduction and the hands-on exercise guidelines for completing the Mural and preparing for the group presentation.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/02/Mural-tiny.png)

Case Study Mural

The workshop's learning objectives were to support partners in positioning and delivering RISE with SAP and to enable them to use SAP Signavio Process Insights as a solution to improve their business processes. This workshop aimed at business architects, innovation managers, and presales consultants looking to drive end-to-end process discussions, digitize and automate processes, and position the entire solution.

SAP Signavio is a key component of the Intelligent Enterprise Strategy. The workshop provided a valuable opportunity for attendees to learn about its capabilities and how it can help their customers transform their businesses.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/02/IESignavio.jpg)

Intelligent Enterprise and SAP Signavio

To learn more about the workshop, you can watch this [video](https://iris.video.sap.com/message/d3a46e6929a196e237ab35ddd3624892f021b9f5cbd663002abb413caf53)
