## The SAP Business Technology Platform

The SAP Business Technology Platform is a key element of SAP's strategy. It is considered to be the foundation of any Intelligent and Sustainable Entperprise. Thus, it's essential for companies going through a Digital Transformation to understand what the SAP Business Technology Platform is and how it can help them extend, integrate, automate and analyze their business.
