Dear hacker,

Any conference is incomplete without the networking aspect.

That’s why — #IWCON2022’s super cool networking rooms will be open for 48 hrs this time!

This way, people from all over the world can join in & make some amazing infosec friends, irrespective of their time zone.

If you’ve registered for the event, join in and start the conversations!

If you haven’t registered yet, [**save your seat now**](https://iwcon.live/)**.**

PS: If you’re curious how a virtual networking session looks like, check out some snippets from the previous version of IWCON here-

## Announcement: We have a new speaker!

We have an amazing new speaker at the last moment!

Paris Stone, Security Advisor at Halborn, an award-winning solutions firm that’s securing the convergence of Web2 & Web3, will be speaking on **What the Hack is an MPC?**

Timing: 8.30 PM IST, 18th December 2022.
