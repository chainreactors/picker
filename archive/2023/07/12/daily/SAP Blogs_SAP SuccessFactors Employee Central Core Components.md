Becoming an Employee Central Certified Associate starts with the **SAP SuccessFactors Configure SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Core** course**.**

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/EC-Structure-Diagram-1.png)

Employee Central Components

The course consists of these critical concepts and the tools that will allow you to configure it:

* **Foundation Objects** – One of the core functions of Employee Central as an HR Information System is to store company information. Foundation Objects allow companies to store organization, pay, and job-related information. Employee Central comes with standard Foundations Objects.

* **HR Data** – Another core HRIS component is storing employee information. The standard Employee Central data model includes person and employment data structure to store HR data. The pre-defined date-effective objects ensure that history and changes to employee records are captured.

* **Role-Based Permissions** –Employee Central uses role-based permission as its security model. Employees, managers, and HRs can initiate transactions based on granted permissions.

* **Workflows, Event Reasons, and Business** Rules – Managing transactions are made more efficient when business rules are used to automate approval and event reasons.

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central drives much of the information used by talent solutions, making it a must-learn for anyone implementing SAP SuccessFactors HCM solutions.
