I've known the [CrypTool](https://www.cryptool.org/) website for many years, I use it occasioanly when I need to do some crypto and I only have a browser at my disposal. Lately, I'm more inclined to use [CyberChef](https://gchq.github.io/CyberChef/), as it provides also many non-crypto features.

Recently I discoved "[Web OpenSSL](https://www.cryptool.org/en/cto/openssl)" on CrypTool: a WebAssembly implementation of OpenSSL.

You can just run it inside your browser:

![](https://isc.sans.edu/diaryimages/images/20230226-182631.png)

Issue commands, and download the files from your browser:
