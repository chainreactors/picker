![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/SAP-Transportation-Management-Overview-3.jpg)

In today's fast-paced business world, the efficient movement of goods is crucial for maintaining a competitive edge. Logistics and transportation play a pivotal role in ensuring timely deliveries, reducing costs, and enhancing customer satisfaction.

SAP Transportation Management (SAP TM) solution helps businesses handle shipping and delivery more efficiently. It integrates fleet and logistics management throughout your network, helping to dramatically reduce transportation complexity. With seamless transportation planning, cost optimization, real-time visibility, collaboration capabilities, and performance measurement, SAP TM helps businesses achieve a sustainable, lean, and agile supply chain.

If you're an SAP Enterprise Support or SAP Enterprise Support cloud customer and want to get insights into SAP Transportation Management, we have great news for you!

I am pleased to share e-Learning: [**SAP Digital Supply Chain - SAP Transportation Management Overview**](https://accounts.sap.com/saml2/idp/sso?sp=https://www.successfactors.eu/learninghub&RelayState=%2Fsf%2Flearning%3FdestUrl%3Dhttps%253a%252f%252fsaplearninghub%252eplateau%252ecom%252flearning%252fuser%252fdeeplink%255fredirect%252ejsp%253flinkId%253dCATALOG%255fSEARCH%2526sbArSel%253d%2526keywords%253dSUP_ELE_0740_2307%2526selKeyWordHeader%253dSUP_ELE_0740_2307%2526catSel%253d%2526srcSel%253dESAC%2526delMthSel%253d%2526ILDateFrm%253d%2526ILDateTo%253d%2526ILBlend%253d%2526ILSchd%253d%2526fromSF%253dY%26company%3Dlearninghub "SAP Digital Supply Chain - SAP Transportation Management Overview")\*.

This e-learning provides an overview of SAP Transportation Management as part of the SAP Digital Supply Chain. It has been updated to include insights into data volume management and archiving strategies.

With the latest update, you will gain insights into SAP Transportation Management, its deployment options, and how Data Volume Management (DVM) can help you manage large data volumes effectively.

SAP Transportation Management solution offers different deployment options:

* a standalone solution that can be integrated into your ERP applications (e.g., SAP ECC,  SAP S/4HANA, and SAP S/4HANA Cloud private/public),
* implemented as part of SAP S/4HANA
* implemented as part of SAP S/4HANA Cloud, private edition

In this e-Learning, you will:

* Get an introduction to SAP Digital Supply Chain.
* Learn about SAP Transportation Management, including solution details, deployment options, and innovations.
* Understand data volume management options for large tables in SAP Transportation Management and explore data archiving options in detail.
* Gain additional insights into SAP Transportation Management and its data management.
