# ALIEN TXTBASE Stealer Logs

[StealerLogs Breach](/FAQs#StealerLogs)

## What Happened

In February 2025, [23 billion rows of stealer logs were obtained from a Telegram channel known as ALIEN TXTBASE](https://www.troyhunt.com/processing-23-billion-rows-of-alien-txtbase-stealer-logs). The data contained 284M unique email addresses alongside the websites they were entered into and the passwords used. This data is now searchable in HIBP by both email domain and the domain of the target website.
