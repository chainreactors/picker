In a promising sign that public pressure works, several crisis pregnancy centers (CPCs, also known as “[fake clinics](https://www.plannedparenthood.org/blog/what-are-crisis-pregnancy-centers)”) have quietly scrubbed misleading language about privacy protections from their websites.
