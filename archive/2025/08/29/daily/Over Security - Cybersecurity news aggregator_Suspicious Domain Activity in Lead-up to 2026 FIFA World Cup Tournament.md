## *The 2025 Club World Cup was a sign of what’s to come for the 2026 World Cup*

## Executive summary

**Date:** August 2025
**Author:** PreCrime Labs, BforeAI

In the lead-up to major global events, cybercriminals are quick to launch fraudulent schemes like fake websites and counterfeit online stores. PreCrime Labs, the threat research team at BforeAI, recently found a sizable number of domains created to take advantage of the FIFA Club World Cup 2025 in the U.S. The observations from this event are a harbinger of the malicious domain activity sponsors, businesses, and fans should expect during next year’s 2026 World Cup tournament, also being hosted in the U.S. (as well as Canada and Mexico).
