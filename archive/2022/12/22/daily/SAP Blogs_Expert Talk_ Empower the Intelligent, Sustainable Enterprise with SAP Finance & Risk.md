Welcome to this blog from katrin.deissner and me.

***"An intelligent, sustainable enterprise** is one that consistently applies advanced technologies and best practices within agile, integrated business processes. SAP can help you build an intelligent, sustainable enterprise by enabling you to:*

* *Employ tools to understand what's happening across your business and why*

* *Infuse AI and machine learning into your business processes*

* *Embed sustainable practices into the core of your business strategy and operations*

* *Build flexible value chains and apply leading-edge industry practices to optimize your business*

* *Implement vertical industry-specific processes in the cloud to run your value chain end to end"\**

Watch this Expert Talk video where Yannick Peterschmitt and his guests from SAP Engineering Katrin Deissner, Dr. Christoph Ernst and Ulrich Hauke discuss how to Empower the Intelligent, Sustainable Enterprise with **SAP Finance and Risk**.

Participants:

* Dr. Christoph Ernst: Head of SAP Finance & Risk Product Management

* Yannick Peterschmitt: Head of SAP S/4HANA Product Success and Cloud Co-Innovation Enablement

* Katrin Deissner: Product Manager for GRC

* Ulrich Hauke: Product Manager for Finance

In this talk you will learn about:

* 01:15 - Strategic Direction and Vision of Finance & Risk: Pillar 1 – Business Transformation

* 03:30 - Innovations in the Space of Augmented Business Steering

* 06:25 - Strategic Direction and Vision of Finance & Risk: Pillar 2 – Supply Chain Resilience

* 07:48 - Innovations in the Space of Supply Chain Resilience: GRC Products

* 13:20 - Finance Innovation: Value Chain Analysis

* 16:05 - Strategic Direction of Finance & Risk: Pillar 3 – Product Vision for Sustainability Outcomes

* 21:45 - Consumption of New Innovations
