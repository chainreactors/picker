# Navigating the future of cybersecurity

## Take a deep dive into the world of emerging cyber threats and defense strategies with Cloudflare

[Annaliese Ingrams](/Author/Annaliese-Ingrams "Read more by this author")

Fri 16 Aug 2024  //
15:02 UTC

![](/design_picker/d2e337b97204af4aa34dda04c4e5d56d954b216f/graphics/icons/social_share_icon.svg)

Webinar In a world where cyber threats are continually evolving, staying informed is critical for IT and security professionals.

Cloudflare's 2024 Global Security Brief offers a thorough examination of these challenges, providing essential insights for safeguarding your organization.

Join this [webinar on August 20th at 12pm ET/9am PT](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/30389/why-informed-is-forearmed-understanding-escalating-threat-trends) when Cloudflare experts Trey Guinn and Trevor Lyness will provide an in-depth analysis of:

**- Advanced DDoS tactics:** Discover the latest trends in Distributed Denial-of-Service attacks, highlighting new strategies and methods.

**- API and network threats:** Learn about the vulnerabilities in APIs and network infrastructure that are being increasingly targeted by cybercriminals.

**- AI-enhanced phishing:** Uncover how artificial intelligence is making phishing attacks more sophisticated and challenging to detect.

**- Zero Trust architecture:** Explore the principles of Zero Trust security and how implementing this model can strengthen your cybersecurity framework.

This session will provide you with actionable knowledge and practical strategies that may help combat the most pressing cyber threats of today and tomorrow.

[Register for the webinar here](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/30389/why-informed-is-forearmed-understanding-escalating-threat-trends) to equip yourself with some of the latest insights and tools needed to navigate the complex cybersecurity landscape.
