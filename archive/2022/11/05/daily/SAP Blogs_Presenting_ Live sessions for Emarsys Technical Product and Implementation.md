Hi SAP Community!

Announcing a live session series for SAP Emarsys!

**Background Context**

SAP Emarsys is a captivating customer engagement solution that empowers marketers to grow their customer connections. This 6 day live session is intended to help upskill new users and demonstrate the key features and benefits of SAP Emarsys.

**What is it about?**

The Emarsys Technical Product and Implementation series will provide you with a deep dive description and application of the product. This series of sessions will cover the key features and benefits of the product. We will discuss how to effectively implement the product.

Lastly, it will demonstrate how to utilize the key areas of the product to ensure successful usage of the product. We’ll review personalization, voucher management, automations, strategic dashboard, tactics, predict and web extend, smart insight and web recommendations.

**Who is it for?**

This live session has been created for those persons who need to learn about SAP Emarsys solution. **Consultants**, **Integration** **Developers**, and **Architects** are the targeted roles.

**What does it offer?**

This is a 6 day course taught by both the Emarsys and SAP training teams. It will cover the technical product information and technical implementation.

**How can you book it?**

This live session is available on [SAP Learning Hub](https://learninghub.sap.com/). You will need to have a subscription. It is also located in [PartnerEdge](https://partneredge.sap.com/en/welcome.html).

**Course Details:**

November  8,9, 10 and 15, 16, 17

Time: 2pm-5pm CET to 4pm-7pm CET

You can click the [link here](https://performancemanager.successfactors.eu/sf/learning?destUrl=https%3a%2f%2fsaplearninghub%2eplateau%2ecom%2flearning%2fuser%2fdeeplink%5fredirect%2ejsp%3flinkId%3dITEM%5fDETAILS%26componentID%3dLS%5fEMARSYSIMP%5fEN%26componentTypeID%3dEXPERT%5fLED%26revisionDate%3d1634627940000%26fromSF%3dY&company=learninghub) to register

For better understanding the live session’s content, it’s recommended you know about the basics regarding SAP Emarsys before or after attending the live session. A great place to start is our Open SAP Microlearning platform located here [SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement - openSAP Microlearning](https://microlearning.opensap.com/category/SAP%2BEmarsys%2BCustomer%2BEngagement/207069023)

**Last But Not Least**

If you want to know more or get informed on any update of this live session, please follow this blog post by clicking on the “follow” green button on the left. You can also contact Cheryl Melville at Cheryl.melville@sap.com
