# Pi-hole

## What Happened

In July 2025, [a vulnerability in the GiveWP WordPress plugin exposed the names and email addresses of approximately 30k donors to the Pi-hole network-wide ad blocking project](https://pi-hole.net/blog/2025/07/30/compromised-donor-emails-a-post-mortem/). Pi-hole subsequently self-submitted the list of impacted donors to HIBP.
