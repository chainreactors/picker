# Cisco Talos at RSAC 2024

By
[Madison Burns](https://blog.talosintelligence.com/author/madison/)

Tuesday, April 30, 2024 08:00

[Announcements](/category/announcements/)

With RSAC just a week away, Cisco Talos is gearing up for another year of heading to San Francisco to share in some of the latest major cybersecurity announcements, research and news.

We’ve pulled together the highlights, so you don’t miss out on all things Talos.

**Tuesday, May 7**

Joe Marshall will be presenting on [Project Power Up](http://project%20power%20up/) alongside Tara Vasyliv of NPC UKRENERGO on how Talos has helped to protect Ukraine’s power grid from disruptive electronic warfare. Reserve your seat for the 8:30 a.m. [session here](https://www.rsaconference.com/USA/agenda/session/Project%20Power%20Up%20Fighting%20Electronic%20Warfare%20to%20Keep%20Ukraines%20Grid%20Safe).

Nick Biasini will be giving a lighting talk at the Cisco Booth N-5845 in the North Hall at 3:30 p.m. on the good, the bad and the ugly of ransomware in 2024.

**Wednesday, May 8**

Nicole Hoffman will be signing her children’s book, “[The Mighty Threat Intelligence Warrior](https://threathuntergirl.com/childrens-book/),” at 12:30 p.m. in the [RSA Bookstore](https://www.rsaconference.com/usa/programs/Bookstore?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_content=bookstore&utm_campaign=onsite-us2023&postID=9646319757) located in Moscone South, Esplanade Level.

**Thursday, May 9**

Hoffman will be out again, this time hosting a session on applying past lessons learned in hopes of a better future for identify threat detection at 9:40 a.m. in Moscone West 3022. Reserve a seat for the [session here](https://www.rsaconference.com/USA/agenda/session/Applying%20Past%20Lessons%20for%20Intel-Driven%20Identity%20Threat%20Detection).

And you can always follow along all week on [X](https://twitter.com/TalosSecurity) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/cisco-talos-intelligence-group/mycompany) for the latest updates.
