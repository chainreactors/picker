Are you a user of one or more SAP products? SAP User Assistance (UA) would LOVE to hear from you about social media!

To better reach you with the how-to news you need to know, UA is boosting its social media presence. We'd love to understand how you use social media in your business life, how you look for information to answer questions and solve problems, and more.

Sign up here to help us help you better! Registration is open through FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 18.

[CEI - Social Media Engagement](https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/campaign/3242)

Registrants will attend a short intro call and fill out a questionnaire. That's it!

We look forward to talking to you!
