**Dear Customers**,

It's our pleasure to invite you to the next [ENLIT 2022](https://www.enlit-europe.com/) taking place from November 29 –                   December 01, 2022 in Frankfurt, Germany.

ENLIT is the largest utilities conference in Europe and SAP will be present as a **Gold Sponsor.**

Visit SAP to get the lateshttps://portal.wdf.sap.corp/homet roadmap details, find out more about SAP Utilities Core, the industry transformations around asset, energy, customer and service and learn how SAP can empower you for the energy transition.

You can meet us at our **meeting corner >12.1.B202 or meeting room >12.1 MR3 on Floor 1**

In addition, mark your calendar for the following highlights:

* Summit – Inspire Session: **“Enablers for the Energy Transition”,** November 29th [>> Link](https://www.enlit-europe.com/programme/summit?&filters.location=3649%2C&filters.date=29-Nov-2022%2C30-Nov-2022%2C01-Dec-2022%2C&sortby=customfield_4984%20asc&filters.STREAM=inspire-stage-2iou&searchgroup=314A95B3-summit)

* Summit – **Interview** **Connect Studio** with Daniela Sellmann, November 29th

* Hub Session by Slawek Klimowicz on November 30th, 10:00 - 10:15 [>>LINK](https://www.enlit-europe.com/speakers/slawomir-klimowicz?&filters.speaker-type=Summit%20Programme%2CHub%20Programme&azletter=K&searchgroup=libraryentry-speakers)

* **Networking Event with SAP & Capgemini,** November 30th
  4:30pm – 6.30pm in the Food Court and Networking Area.

**Find the Programme at a glance online**: [>>HERE.](https://cdn.asp.events/CLIENT_CL_EE_E92EC48A_9F42_8E1E_7106D5CAFEEF513B/sites/Enlit-Europe-2022/media/Pages/programme/EnlitEurope2022_Programme_Grid.pdf)

Save the date and register today for this special event.

Use this link to **register for the event for free** and/or attend the Summit at a discounted rate of 25%! [Register Here!](https://www.enlit-europe.com/invite-sap)

We look forward seeing you in Frankfurt!

Melanie

Marketing & Communications Lead, IBU Utilities
