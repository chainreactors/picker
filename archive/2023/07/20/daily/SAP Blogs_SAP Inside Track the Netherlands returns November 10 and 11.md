Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to either:

* Host a session to share your experiences with your peers

* Join to learn from your peers

Fun will be included ![:grinning_face_with_smiling_eyes:](/html/@76EAE36C4E2C5DDAE7C75D1E3BBF3E18/emoticons/1f604.png ":grinning_face_with_smiling_eyes:")

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/sitNL-2023-v0.1.png)

Super that we can organise this 13th episode together with VNSG, the Dutch SAP User Group.

Our program will cover two days:

* Friday offers two masterclasses, handson sessions, with SAP Datasphere and SAP Build.

* Saturday is where the SAP community reunites.

Location will be SAP NL HQ in ‘s-Hertogenbosch.

Registration for the event will open soon. But we are already open to [receive your session proposals](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe-m8HtvA3CoPgS8mwpThuxE2b-HfiYYeqTm_RcYaY2C8-14A/viewform).

Any questions or suggestions, please let us know.

Your #sitNL organisation crew.

Wim, Jan, Ronald, Tamas, Roel, Caspár, Coen and Twan
