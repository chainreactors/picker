Hi SAP Community!

I'm announcing the last instance of the SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement Onboarding sessions in 2022.

### Background Context

SAP Emarsys is an effective and straight-to-the-point solution that empowers marketers to increase their customer engagement capabilities.

This 3-day Live Session will present a first onboarding and an overview of SAP Emarsys for beginner-level audiences.

### What is it about?

The SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement Onboarding sessions will offer you a hands-on experience through the main functionalities. You will be guided by a real-life Use Case to which a solution will be designed - and built by you throughout the exercises, step by step.

### Who is it for?

This live session is designed for Marketing Managers, Consultants, Partners, and other roles involved in day-to-day marketing operations.

### What does it offer?

This is a 3-day (6 hours in total) instructor-led hands-on session. We offer you:

* Access to a training account (after registration)

* An instructor to answer course-related questions

* Lots of interactivity, to keep everyone engaged

### How can you book it?

This live session is available on [SAP Learning Hub](https://learninghub.sap.com/). You will need to have a subscription.

You can [click here](https://saplearninghub.plateau.com/learning/user/common/viewItemDetails.do?OWASP_CSRFTOKEN=LR28-JYZZ-YI9A-MEA7-Z2Z0-0Y6Z-TFKL-YXXT&componentID=LS_EMARYS_EN&componentTypeID=EXPERT_LED&fromSF=Y&revisionDate=1620201240000&hideItemDetailsBackLink=y&hideItemDetailsBackLink=true&menuGroup=Learning&menuItem=Cur&fromDeepLink=true) to register for this session.

### Course Details

Date: From **29 November** to **1 December** 2022

Time: From **11:00 AM** to **1:00 PM**, Ameryca/New York time (GMT -5)

### Last but not least

For a better understanding of this session and what it is about, we recommend you to watch the Overview videos in the [SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement - openSAP Microlearning](https://microlearning.opensap.com/category/SAP%2BEmarsys%2BCustomer%2BEngagement/207069023) portal.

If you want to know more about this training, or if you want to get informed on any updates of this live session, please follow this blog post by clicking on the "follow" green button on the left.

If you have any questions, feel free to add them as a comment to this post.
