# Fédération Francaise de Rugby

## What Happened

In June 2023, [the Fédération Francaise de Rugby (French Rugby Federation) suffered a data breach and attempted ransom](https://www.lemonde.fr/sport/article/2023/06/22/des-pirates-informatiques-tentent-de-faire-chanter-la-federation-francaise-de-rugby-victime-d-une-attaque_6178763_3242.html). The breach exposed 282k unique email addresses along with names, dates of birth and phone numbers. [The Federation subsequently published a disclosure notice](https://www.ffr.fr/actualites/federation/attaque-informatique-des-reseaux-ffr) and stated that the attack primarily affected email servers.
