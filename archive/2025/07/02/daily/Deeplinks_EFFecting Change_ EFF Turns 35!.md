[We're wishing EFF a happy birthday on July 10!](https://www.eff.org/35) Since 1990, EFF's lawyers, activists, analysts, and technologists have used everything in their toolkit to ensure that technology supports freedom, justice, and innovation for all people of the world. They've seen it all and in this special edition of our EFFecting Change livestream series, leading experts at EFF will explore what's next for technology users.

***EFFecting Change Livestream Series:
EFF Turns 35!
Thursday, July 10th
11:00 AM - 12:00 PM Pacific - [Check Local Time](https://dateful.com/eventlink/1636667650)
This event is LIVE and FREE!***

***[![RSVP Today](https://www.eff.org/files/2024/03/20/rsvptoday_0.png)](https://www.eff.org/event/effecting-change-eff-turns-35)***

Join EFF [Executive Director Cindy Cohn](https://www.eff.org/event/effecting-change-eff-turns-35#Cindy), EFF [Legislative Director Lee Tien](https://www.eff.org/event/effecting-change-eff-turns-35#Lee), EFF [Director for International Freedom of Expression Jillian C. York](https://www.eff.org/event/effecting-change-eff-turns-35#Jillian), and [Professor / EFF Board Member Yoshi Kohno](https://www.eff.org/event/effecting-change-eff-turns-35#Yoshi) for this live Q&A. Learn what they have seen and how we can fuel the fight for privacy, free expression, and a future where digital freedoms are protected for everyone.
