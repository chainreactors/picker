# Hello, World — Sandfly 1.1 is now available

18 April 2018

Product Update

After much time coding and testing, we are pleased to announce that Sandfly 1.1 is now released.

Sandfly is an agentless security investigator and compromise detection system for Linux. With Sandfly you can have an instant security presence on your Linux systems without the hassle of installing and maintaining agents. Are you hunting rootkits, or are rootkits hunting you? Let Sandfly tell you.

Find out more on our [product page](/product/how-sandfly-works/).
