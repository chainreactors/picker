Two recent statements from the surveillance company—one [addressing Illinois privacy violations](https://www.flocksafety.com/articles/flock-safetys-response-to-illinois-lpr-data-use-and-out-of-state-sharing-concerns) and another [defending the company's national surveillance network](https://www.flocksafety.com/articles/statement-network-sharing-use-cases-federal-cooperation)—reveal a troubling pattern: when confronted by evidence of widespread abuse, Flock Safety has blamed users, downplayed harms, and doubled down on the very systems that enabled the violations in the first place.

Flock's aggressive public relations campaign to salvage its reputation comes as no surprise.
