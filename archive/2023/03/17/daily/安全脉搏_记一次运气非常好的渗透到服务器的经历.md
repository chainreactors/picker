# 记一次运气非常好的渗透到服务器的经历

[蚁景网安实验室](https://www.secpulse.com/newpage/author?author_id=37244)
![]( https://www.secpulse.com/wp-content/themes/secpulse2017/img/renzheng2.png)

2023-03-16

9,171

平平无奇的客服平台

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932811.png "null")

这个客服平台是有RCE的，如果上传到的不是oss服务器，存储在本地服务器的话

在返回端口的url是存在st2

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932814.png "null")
![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-16789328141.png "null")

root权限，由于是客服后台服务器，没有啥有用价值的信息

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932818.png "null")

直接替换私钥连服务器

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932819.png "null")

继续翻找有用的信息

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932824.png "null")
![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-16789328241.png "null")

配置文件里也只有mongodb和redis的连接信息

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932825.png "null")

历史命令和登录ip历史是阿里云服务器

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932830.png "null")

扫下端口，8092有个目录遍历，好像是专门用来放项目的

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932831.png "null")

点开test看看

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-16789328311.png "null")

下载过来解包看看

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932832.png "null")

这咋有个ip，root和密码，不会是那个开发人才留下的吧，泪目了家人们。

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932833.png "null")

尝试连接

还真可以

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-16789328331.png "null")

连接数据库看看，8w多受害者，佩服

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932835.png "null")

然后再回到之前扫出来的端口

8094是有个web项目

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932837.png "null")

在数据库获取到后台url

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932838.png "null")

配合数据库和登录日志获取到国内技术嫌疑人

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932848.png "null")
![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932849.jpeg "null")

Ip和经纬度是在国内。

总结一下，下班

![](https://secpulseoss.oss-cn-shanghai.aliyuncs.com/wp-content/uploads/1970/01/beepress-image-197623-1678932849.png "null")
