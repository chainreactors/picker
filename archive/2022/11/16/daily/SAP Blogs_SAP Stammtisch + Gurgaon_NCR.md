Greetings, SAP Community members.

After an engaging SAP Stammtisch PLUS held on October 28th at SAP Gurgaon (Read - [SAP Stammtisch Plus Gurgaon – Great networking and learning opportunity | SAP Blogs](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/11/11/sap-stammtisch-plus-gurgaon-great-networking-and-learning-opportunity/)), we are back again on November 18th, 2022 same place - same time, with new topics, a brand new event announcement, and a larger SAP community audience.

Anyone and everyone interested in SAP Community topics, be it SAP users, developers, consultants, architects, or students – come, join us, and bring your friends too!

**Link to register**: [SAP Stammtisch PLUS - Gurgaon Tickets, Fri, Nov 18, 2022 at 3:00 PM | Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/sap-stammtisch-plus-gurgaon-tickets-466761725887?aff=ebdssbdestsearch)

**Time & Location: 03:00PM – 05:30PM,  SAP Gurgaon, Vatika Towers, Tower B Cafeteria, Sector 54, Golf Course Road, Gurgaon**

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/11/Draft-18th-Nov-Agenda.jpg)

Looking forward to seeing you all!!

Please share your comments or any queries using the comment box. I will be more than happy to respond.

For anything related to RISE with SAP as a topic, please check [RISE with SAP | SAP | SAP Blogs](https://blogs.sap.com/tags/1e76886f-86ac-4839-9833-8bf95f5eb775/)

For more information related to Stammtisch Gurgaon and SAP Inside track Gurgaon events, please follow my profile page.
