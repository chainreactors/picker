At the occasion of the launch of the new version of our platform [Stalkphish.io](http://Stalkphish.io) – our SaaS platform dedicated to detection and investigation of phishing/brand impersonation – we launch our first paid plan.

## What’s new for this new plan

This new plan allows you to retrieve more data, more often and more enriched.

The data retrieved with the new plan contain several new features as **SSL/TLS certificate data** of the URL, the **GoogleSafeBrowsing** state at the time of detection and a **phishing score**.

This additional data has been collected for several months or even years and is therefore available for older URLs.

Here is an example of data retrieved with the Standard plan:

![](https://stalkphish.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/stalkphish-api-news2.png?w=1024)

REST API data retrieved with the Standard plan

This plan, in addition to the searches that can be performed in the Free plan, will allow you to perform **string searches in the title of the pages** and to be able to perform **50 API queries per day instead** of the 10 queries available in the Free plan.

Also, each query in the Standard plan will **return 100 results** instead of the 50 in the Free plan.

> More data, more requests, more volume for 700€/month (7700€/year)
