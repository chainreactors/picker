* **Brand protection is the proactive safeguarding of a company’s name, logo, and other digital assets against counterfeit initiatives such as phishing or spoofing.**

* **An effective protection strategy reduces financial losses and maintains customer trust by addressing online threats before a cybercrime occurs.**

* **BforeAI’s PreCrime™ platform offers advanced predictive security solutions to increase consumer confidence and eliminate disruptions to business operations.**

Brand protection is vital for safeguarding reputation, intellectual property, and customer trust. Online brand attacks such as counterfeiting and phishing, which can total millions of dollars in damages, are increasing each year. Because a single bad experience can destroy consumer trust, top enterprises invest in brand protection to maintain loyalty, market share, and a positive online presence.
