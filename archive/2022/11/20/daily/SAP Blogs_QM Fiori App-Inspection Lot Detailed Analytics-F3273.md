**Purpose:** This blog post is about  an excellent SAP QM app  on Analysis of Inspection Lots

SAP Help: [Fiori Browser](https://fioriappslibrary.hana.ondemand.com/sap/fix/externalViewer/#/detail/Apps('F3273')/S21OP)

[App Documentation](https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_S4HANA_ON-PREMISE/2bc3ee8d1c83404e8cf62418640004f2/9215466bd81143dab8ad22a8929459d1.html?version=2021.000)

**Features of  the App** (Do refer to detailed documentation)

With this app, you analyze your inspection lots for which usage decisions have already been made for categories such as accepted and rejected lots, rejection rate, skip rate, total number of lots, skip lots, and mean processing time based on time, inspection type, material, plant, supplier, and customer.
