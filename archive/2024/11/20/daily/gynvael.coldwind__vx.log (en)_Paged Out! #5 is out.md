## [Paged Out! #5 is out](?id=797)

paged out

[![](img/po5_banner.jpg)](https://pagedout.institute/)

**Issue #5 of Paged Out!** is out! Here are the most important links:

* [Paged Out! #5 PDF](https://pagedout.institute/download/PagedOut_005.pdf) (23MB, release build)
* [Official #5 Wallpaper](https://pagedout.institute/download/PagedOut_005_wallpaper.jpg) (10MB, JPG, 8K); there's a PNG on the website as well
* [Paged Out! Website](https://pagedout.institute/)
* [Paged Out! blogpost about #5](https://pagedout.institute/?page=blog.php#entry-2024-11-19)
* and most importantly [Call for Pages](https://pagedout.institute/?page=cfp.php) for Issue #6.

We have some amazing articles and art (finally!) for you in this issue – there are 68 pages altogether (including 2 by yours truly). Here's a high-level list of topics (in alphabetic order):

* Art,
* Algorithms,
* Artificial Intelligence,
* Cryptography,
* File Formats,
* GameDev,
* Hardware,
* History,
* Networks,
* OS Internals,
* Operating Systems,
* Programming,
* Retro,
* Reverse Engineering,
* and Security/Hacking.

Anyway, if you'd like to be informed about Issue #6 once it comes out, here are some ways to achieve that:

* You can use an [RSS](https://pagedout.institute/rss.xml)/[Atom](https://pagedout.institute/atom.xml) reader.
* You can join [this e-mail group (Google Groups)](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/pagedout-notifications).
* Or you check us out on social media – we have it all! You can join our [Discord server](https://gynvael.coldwind.pl/discord), follow us on [X/Twitter](https://twitter.com/pagedout_zine), [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/pagedout.bsky.social), or [Mastodon](https://infosec.exchange/%40PagedOut).
