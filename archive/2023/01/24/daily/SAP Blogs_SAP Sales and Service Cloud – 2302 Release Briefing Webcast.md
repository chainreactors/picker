Dear Community,

The 2302 release (February 2023) for SAP Sales and Service Cloud is approaching.

We will be adding the release webcast-related information and assets here in our community. This will allow you to access the on-demand recording after the live event, short demos if available, slide deck, and also keep the conversation alive.

[**SAP Sales and Service Cloud – 2302 Release Briefing Webcast**](https://gateway.on24.com/wcc/eh/2983783/lp/4053543/sap-sales-and-service-cloud-2302-release-briefing?partnerref=community-blog)

When: Thursday, February 2, 2022 @ 4.00 pm – 5.30 pm CEST/ 10.00am -11:30 am EDT.
Who: Presented by Emmanuel Desert and Sales & Service Cloud Product Managers
What: Get an insightful preview on the planned innovations for the upcoming February 2023 release (2302) of SAP Sales and Service Cloud. Hear directly from our product management team about what’s new in this release. We’ll talk about highlights in Platform & Integration, Sales, and Service.

**Watch the full [Webcast Replay Here](https://gateway.on24.com/wcc/eh/2983783/lp/4053543/sap-sales-and-service-cloud-2302-release-briefing?partnerref=community-blog).**

Please bookmark our [2023 release schedule located here](https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_CLOUD_FOR_CUSTOMER/815c8746048548f49a62406258083385/49b90ed306c9438eb8d131420f058867.html).

**Highlights for the 2302 Release:**

**Platform & Integration**

**MS Teams Integration**

* Deal Room For Account

* Lifecycle Management Deal Room for Opportunity

**SAP Sales and Service Cloud Core - Sales**

* Opportunity: Copy parent opportunity into child hierarchy

* Sales Quote: Additional approver parties

* Contract: Archiving

* Business Partner: New business configuration question for employee working hours maintenance

* Perfect Store: Copy KPI within engagement map

* Groupware Integration: Additional language support in server side integration

**SAP Sales and Service Core, Dynamic Visit Planning Add-On**

* Supporting other frequency types including daily and monthly in route groups

* Supporting mass edit in route groups

* Perfect Store indicator and mass edit at visit list level and at route groups account level

**SAP Sales and Service Core, Intelligent Sales Add-On**

* Customer Insights: Generic KPIs and i-flows

* Guided Selling: Embedded Guided Selling

* Guided Selling: Launch Kanban View from Opportunity Dataset

* Guided Selling: Kanban View – Quick view and opportunity details page
