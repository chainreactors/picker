**Published**: 2025-05-03. **Last Updated**: 2025-05-03 07:23:30 UTC
**by** [Didier Stevens](/handler_list.html#didier-stevens) (Version: 1)

[2 comment(s)](/diary/Steganography%2BChallenge/31910/#comments)

If you are interested in experimenting with steganography and my tools, I propose the following challenge.

[This GitHub project](https://github.com/auyer/steganography) is for a steganography tool. It has a [PNG image of a stegosaurus](https://github.com/auyer/steganography/blob/main/examples/encoded_stegosaurus.png) with an encoded message.

The challenge is to use my tools to decode the message.

The steganographic algortihm is a bit different than the one I described in diary entry "[Steganography Analysis With pngdump.py: Bitstreams](https://isc.sans.edu/diary/Steganography%20Analysis%20With%20pngdump.py%3A%20Bitstreams/31904)".

If you are stuck and need a hint (ROT13): zngevk genafcbfvgvba

Next Saturday, I will post my solution.
