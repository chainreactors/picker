In addition to the already established in-app help, we are delighted to now provide even more in-depth information about SAP Cloud ALM in the form of our brand-new application help on SAP Help Portal.

### [SAP Cloud ALM - Application Help](https://help.sap.com/docs/cloud-alm/applicationhelp)

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/Blog-Application_Help-FirstPage-1.png)

## What's special about it?

The structure of the document mirrors that of the SAP Cloud ALM launchpad - each section (Implementation, Operations, and Service) contains subsections that correspond to the individual applications.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/Blog-Application_Help2-1.png)

For each application, you can then find comprehensive context descriptions about its functionalities, along with step-by-step guidance on how to accomplish your tasks.

Interactive flow charts, graphics, and embedded videos illustrate end-to-end procedures and the interactions between different SAP Cloud ALM applications. Useful links to relevant product documentation, SAP Notes, and KBAs help you find related information.

On top of this, all information in this guide is available in 7 languages:

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/Blog-Application_Help-Languages-2.png)

## Tell us what you think!

As SAP Cloud ALM grows, so will the application help. We are continually working on extending the existing information, on documenting new features, and on making your overall experience more engaging and productive.

Your feedback is what drives and shapes better documentation. If you have improvement ideas or want to provide general feedback for this guide, simply answer the question "Was this page helpful?" and give further information in the free text field at the right-hand side of each help page:

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/Blog-Application_Help-Feedback2-1.png)
