## Executive Summary: 7 Tips for Brand Protection Enforcement

* **Brand protection enforcement** is a preemptive strategy against the unauthorized use or infringement of your brand’s intellectual property.
* **Effective brand protection** requires a mix of legal registration, clear internal policies, and operational vigilance across digital channels.
* **Leveraging AI and advanced monitoring tools** is critical for staying ahead of threats and automating enforcement processes.

If someone uses your brand name or logo without permission, that’s trademark infringement. Surely there will be swift consequences, right?

Yet if you’ve spent any time online, you know that copyright violations and brand impersonations are rampant. Unfortunately, by the time your business discovers the misuse and pursues legal action, the reputational damage may already be done. Organizations are increasingly taking their own preemptive measures to uncover phishing scams, unauthorized uses of intellectual property, domain squatting, or other brand impersonation tactics.
