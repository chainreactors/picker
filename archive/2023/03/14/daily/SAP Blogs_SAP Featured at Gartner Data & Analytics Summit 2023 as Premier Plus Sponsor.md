SAP is gearing up for the Gartner Data & Analytics Summit 2023, happening March 20-22 in Orlando, Florida, and we are honoured to take part as a premier plus sponsor.

This conference brings together a global community of experts to address the most significant challenges facing data and analytics leaders today as they build the innovative and adaptable organizations of the future. The event also highlights how businesses across industries are transforming uncertainty into opportunity with data and analytics tools.

We are thrilled to showcase our newest SAP data and analytics solution, SAP Datasphere, that we recently launched on March 8th, 2023. Visit us at booth #424 to learn more about SAP Datasphere and our other SAP data and analytics solutions.

* [SAP Datasphere](https://www.sap.com/products/technology-platform/datasphere.html) - a comprehensive data service that enables every data professional to deliver seamless and scalable access to mission-critical business data

* [SAP HANA Cloud](https://www.sap.com/hanacloud) - a modern database powering the next generation of intelligent data applications and analytics across all enterprise data

* [SAP Analytics Cloud](https://www.sap.com/products/technology-platform/cloud-analytics.html) - an intuitive solution that brings together planning and analytics to make decisions without doubt

As well as integration, governance and master data management solutions.

# Attend SAP hosted Sessions

Join us at these three SAP hosted sessions during the summit.

## How Glanbia Drives Better Financial Decisions with Trusted, Authoritative Data with SAP

### Tuesday, March 21st at 11:00am ET, in Southern Hemisphere V

CFOs and their teams are under increased pressure to become strategic business partners who deliver meaningful business insights. When making data-driven decisions, you need to make sure you have the complete overview of how your business is performing and provide accurate financial analysis and advice. Join this session to learn how **Glanbia**, world’s leader in performance and lifestyle nutrition, gained a complete overview of their business by connecting data across cloud, on-prem, and flat files, to generate more meaningful financial insights to maximize the impact of decisions on the bottom line.

## Simplify access to all data with a Business Data Fabric

### Monday, March 20th at 2:15pm ET, in Europe 2

A Business Data Fabric? Is a semantically-rich layer over a customer’s underlying data components that maintains business context that provides business users with seamless and scalable access to data without duplication.  Providing an environment where business innovation is no longer constrained by where data resides, slowed down by the development of new data staging plans, or hindered by incomplete business context.  Participate in this session with SAP experts to discuss the universal needs of a business data fabric architecture.

## Agile Data & Analytics Governance

### Tuesday, March 21st at 3:00pm ET, in Europe 2

This moderated roundtable session will cover a variety of practices for data quality and governance, including the use of data catalogs for data asset visibility, how to maintain business context and application logic, high-quality business glossaries, automated data lineage, centralized rules & policy management, and more. Join our experts as they share their insights on what’s working best with data & analytics governance.
