## New Header Fields for Payment Advice Documents

We are always thriving to enhance Document Information Extraction service to provide you with the best solution for your business processes. The new header fields for the payment advice document type are now available.

The new header fields:

* Customer Tax ID (tax identifier of the organization sending the payment advice)

* Sender Address

* Sender Name

Please note: The data in the fields for Tax ID, Sender Address and Sender Name enable the data enrichment functionality ([tutorial](https://developers.sap.com/mission.cp-aibus-extract-document-enrich-data.html)) for [Business Entities](https://help.sap.com/docs/DOCUMENT_INFORMATION_EXTRACTION/5fa7265b9ff64d73bac7cec61ee55ae6/044e52fd345a4250b9682dbebe3a09c0.html?q=business%20entities).
