# Ualabee

## What Happened

In May 2025, the South American mobility services platform [Ualabee had hundreds of thousands of records scraped from an interface on their platform](https://news.ualabee.com/Aclaraci-n-sobre-datos-vinculados-a-Ualabee-20f75bdc4ce88041916bfeb5055087e8). The data included 472k unique email addresses along with names, profile photos, dates of birth and phone numbers.
