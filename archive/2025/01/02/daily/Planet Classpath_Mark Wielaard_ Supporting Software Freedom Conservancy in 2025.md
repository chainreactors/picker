## Supporting Software Freedom Conservancy in 2025

Posted on January 1, 2025, 17:58.

![Lots of vintage sfconservancy t-shirts](https://sfconservancy.org/static/img/2020-12-04_lots-vintage-shirts.jpg)

The [Software Freedom Conservancy](https://sfconservancy.org/news/2024/nov/26/2024-fundraiser/) Fundraiser runs for another 2 weeks. *Please become a [Sustainer](https://sfconservancy.org/sustainer/), renew your existing membership or [donate](https://sfconservancy.org/donate/) before January 15th to maximize your contribution to furthering the goals of software freedom!*

They have been a great partner to [Sourceware](https://sourceware.org/mission.html), putting users, developers and community first.
