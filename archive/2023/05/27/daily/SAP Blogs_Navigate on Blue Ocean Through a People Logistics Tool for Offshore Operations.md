Managing the recruitment of skilled professionals, supervising their certificates and training as well as strategizing staff allocation based on project needs, and in the end, ensuring budget compliance it's a mission that even the most skilled manager has struggled to perform. Consider now, all of it in an Upstream project! It's insane.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/05/Captura-de-Tela-2023-05-25-as-17.00.30.png)

Designed to address the specific situation outlined below, **Intcom's BlueOpex** suite was developed to bridge the digitalization gap in People Logistics and Staff Management for the Energy and Maritime sector.

In **2018**, Intcom was chosen to participate in the **SAP Startups Focus program**, allowing the company to gain a deeper knowledge of SAP solutions and strengthen its relationship with SAP.

In the following years, in collaboration with SAP,¬† Intcom, in partnership with SAP, designed an architecture that would enable integration with **SAP's cloud-based solutions**.

##### ![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/05/Captura-de-Tela-2023-05-25-as-16.44.37.png)**BlueOpex positioning integration among SAP environment**

Intcom's BlueOpex is deployed in over 20 companies, including oil companies, large shipowners, and subsea contractors, resulting in about 30% of operational cost reductions.

**Intcom's BlueOpex** is a **BTP** application that allows direct integration with the **S/4HANA****CLOUD** and **SUCCESS FACTORS** solutions and is available in the SAP Store.

[Visit SAP Store](https://store.sap.com/dcp/en/product/display-0000043997_live_v1/BlueOpex) for more information.
