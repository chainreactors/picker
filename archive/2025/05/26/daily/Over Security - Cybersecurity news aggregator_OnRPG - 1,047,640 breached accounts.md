# OnRPG

## What Happened

In July 2016, the now defunct free online games list website OnRPG suffered a data breach [that was later redistributed as part of a larger corpus of data](https://cybernews.com/security/billions-passwords-credentials-leaked-mother-of-all-breaches/). The incident exposed just over 1M email and IP addresses alongside usernames and passwords stored as salted MD5 hashes.
