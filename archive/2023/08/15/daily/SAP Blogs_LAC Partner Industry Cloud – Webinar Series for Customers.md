SAP’s industry cloud is the open innovation space for SAP and our partners, to build differentiating solutions for the core business of our customers. Industry cloud solutions extend the end-to-end processes of SAP S/4HANA, LoB Cloud solutions and the SAP Business Network to enable our customers to optimize and transform their core business. Industry cloud solutions are cloud native and built on SAP Business Technology Platform using intelligent technologies such as analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to create new user experiences that unlock new levels of efficiency and differentiation.

SAP partners are critical to the SAP strategy and industry cloud effort. The partner industry cloud solutions offer a huge value proposition to our customers by bringing innovation and complementary features to market for any industry..

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/08/Strategy.png)To better expose those partners solutions, who complement and support SAP strategy on LAC region, we are running a **weekly webinars series in Spanish and Portuguese language**.

If you are interest to learn more about those solutions, please just register on the webinars above and see how SAP can provide an end-to-end solution for your industry.
