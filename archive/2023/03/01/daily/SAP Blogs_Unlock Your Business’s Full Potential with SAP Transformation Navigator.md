Do you want tofuture-proof your business and bring your IT landscape up to speed? Then it's time to discover the magic of SAP Transformation Navigator. This free, web-based tool is your guide on the journey towards a digital future, providing you with a roadmap to close the gap between your current state and desired outcome.

"Extend Business" is a key feature of SAP Transformation Navigator that lets you take your business processes and capabilities to the next level. With this tool, you'll have the power to identify new business opportunities, pursue them with confidence, and stay ahead of the curve with the latest technologies and innovations. Whether you're looking to improve efficiency, reduce costs, or enhance customer experiences, STN has got you covered.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/02/MicrosoftTeams-image-2.png)

**Ready to see the benefits for yourself? Here's a sneak peek of what you can expect:**

1. Enhanced Business Capabilities: With the ability to extend your SAP landscape, you'll have a competitive edge in the digital world, staying ahead of the curve (and your competitors) with the latest technologies and innovations.

2. Future-Proof Success: Anticipate future market trends and changes in technology with the help of "Extend Business," positioning your business for long-term success.

3. Streamlined Processes: Say goodbye to outdated processes and hello to increased efficiency, reduced costs, and improved customer experiences.

4. Flexibility at Your Fingertips: Quickly respond to changing market conditions and customer needs with the flexibility to extend your SAP systems as needed.

5. Reduced Risks: The SAP Transformation Navigator takes the guesswork out of extending your business processes and capabilities, reducing the risk of project failure and minimizing the potential for errors.

With SAP Transformation Navigator, you'll have all the tools you need to take your organization to the next level of digital transformation. Don't wait any longer to unlock your business's full potential.

Start your journey towards a new digital future today!

Transformation Navigator: <https://go.support.sap.com/transformationnavigator/#/welcome>
