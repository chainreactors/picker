## 下载与安装

首先是下载与安装，官方提供下述操作系统：

* windows
* linux（Debain、Ubuntu、RetHat、Fedora、FreeBSD i386等）
* macOS
* android

## 基本使用

我们安装完WinHTTrack website copier之后，打开软件看到的界面是这样子的：

[![网页访问速度慢？用HTTrack自己制作一个镜像网站吧！](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-2.png)](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-2.png)

下面我们以克隆kafka官网为例：

首先在初始页面点击下一步，进入到如下页面：

[![网页访问速度慢？用HTTrack自己制作一个镜像网站吧！](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-3.png)](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-3.png)

在工程名输入框中输入kafka，在保存路径中选择镜像的保存地址，之后再次点击下一步，得到如下页面：

[![网页访问速度慢？用HTTrack自己制作一个镜像网站吧！](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-4.png)](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-4.png)

在Web地址中输入：[https://kafka.apache.org](https://blog.upx8.com/go/aHR0cHM6Ly9rYWZrYS5hcGFjaGUub3JnLw)

然后，点击选项设定中的选项，可以设置代理等信息，具体如下：

[![网页访问速度慢？用HTTrack自己制作一个镜像网站吧！](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-5.png)](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-5.png)

通过上述设置，我们再次点击下一步，就可以开始对kafka原网站进行克隆，克隆过程如下：

[![网页访问速度慢？用HTTrack自己制作一个镜像网站吧！](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-6.png)](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-6.png)

等到克隆完成，我们可以在本地直接打开该网站或者将其部署到http服务器上。

[![网页访问速度慢？用HTTrack自己制作一个镜像网站吧！](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-7.png)](https://obs-githubhelper.obs.cn-east-3.myhuaweicloud.com/blog-images/tools/httrack/httrack-7.png)
