Dear reader,

Do you know how [InfoSec WriteUps Publication](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/) grew to what it is today?

Every day since December 2017, our creator, [Sai Krishna Kothapalli](https://medium.com/u/be0f09f27ea3) used to send private notes on every great Infosec-related story on Medium.

The screenshot you see here is just one of 12396598475982374 that helped shape the publication so far.

Press enter or click to view image in full size

![]()

Day after day, one story after another, one writer at a time — and we reached where we are today.

I’m sharing this because people often see the end result, not the years of hard work, sweat and tears spent in building something incredible.

Today, we are a community of 26,000+ strong, very soon organizing version 2.0 of our much-loved virtual cybersecurity event: [**IWCON 2022**](https://iwcon.live/): Infosec Writeup’s Virtual Cybersecurity conference and networking event on **17 and 18th December 2022**.

We have 16 amazing speakers (check our line-up [*here*](https://iwcon.live/)) and 2+ hours of virtual one-on-one and group networking sessions.

As someone who has always dreamt of bringing the Infosec community together, this is my first attempt at providing you some lifelong value.

Excited? Book your tickets [**here**](https://razorpay.com/payment-button/pl_K8cxPtmUyBH2PC/view).

The event will see 100s of cybersecurity engineers, students, and bug bounty hunters. If you’ve ever been curious about Infosec, this is your chance to hop in and learn something truly life-changing.

Don’t forget to share this with all your CTF buddies here so they can join in on all the fun!

Hope to see you over at IWCON 2022.

Best,
Editorial team
