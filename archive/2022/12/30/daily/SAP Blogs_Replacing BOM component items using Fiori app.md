The purpose of the blog post is to explain the use of the Fiori app -Material where used list in BOM. In classical SAP, we used CS20 to replace BOM items in mass.

**Step 1**- Access the Fiori app

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/12/29_01.png)

**Step 2**- Enter the component and press the GO button to find in which all BOMs this component was used.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/12/29_02.png)

**Step 3**: - Click Replace material after selecting BOMs (Do see other useful options in the Menu)

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/12/29_03.png)

**Step 4**: Enter the replacement component, quantity, and change number to be used for replacement.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/12/29_04.png)

**Step 5**: Confirm replacement- See the message against each BOM.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/12/29_05.png)

Simple but very powerful app by SAP. Do give feedback to post more such blog posts
