If you are enthusiastic fan of SAP Business ByDesign and our great easy-to-use capability of **Business Cards Scanning**, we have a great news for you!

You can scan now and import business cards information directly also in **four additional languages**.

If you would like to scan in **Hebrew, Chinese, Korean or Japanese** language you are now ready to go, all you need is latest SAP Business ByDesign **2211 release**.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/10/blog-pic.png)

Enjoy!
