Hello dear writers

We at Infosec Writeups are happy to announce that we are opening up paid writing gigs for writers.

Please note some details:

* Youll be paid on a per-article basis.
* This is a long-term gig, and if we like your work, we will keep sending more gigs your way.
* We look for two things: Technical acumen and blog writing skills. If you have the perfect combination of both, [***fill this form***](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScoqdvFBaJ21TwOVD_1M9fdjtQoQQ404OImTj_8qVXSDn_oKA/viewform?usp=sf_link) and confirm your application to the paid writing gigs.

Please share this post with writers who might be interested. The editorial team is very excited to devise another way to serve our community of writers and readers.

For any queries, hit me up at anangsha@infosecwriteups.com.

See you on the other side.

[***Fill this form to apply for paid infosec writing gigs.***](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScoqdvFBaJ21TwOVD_1M9fdjtQoQQ404OImTj_8qVXSDn_oKA/viewform?usp=sf_link)
