![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/12/WhatsApp-Image-2022-12-11-at-3.49.24-PM.jpeg)Hello SAP Enthusiasts ,

SAP Stammtisch Bengaluru is back ! This time it’s an informal meet up in a brewery near Indira Nagar .

**Who can attend ?**

SAP Developers,  Functional consultants, Project managers and architects  - come and join us and bring a friend. It is also open to anyone else that touches SAP in some ways  interested in hearing your thoughts on all things SAP.

**What is SAP Stammtisch Bengaluru ?**

SAP Stammtisch is just a regular meetup of the local SAP community.  The idea started in Germany, hence the German name, It has since spread around the world, with the second taking place in India in 2022

**When and Where:**

**Date:** 22-12-2022

**Timings**: 6:30 PM to 08:30 PM

**Venue:**

Loft 38 ,Shop No, 763, 100 Feet Rd, next to Xtreme Sports Bar, HAL 2nd Stage, Appareddipalya, Indiranagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560038

Google maps link - <https://goo.gl/maps/Ha3UUt6wUsCjwBpE8>

[**Link for the Registration :**](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/sap-stammtisch-bengaluru-india-tickets-484396361557)

**Note:** In case of any change in the location, we will keep you posted by updating the above information.

**Agenda:**

Socializing and future planning of SAP community events.

Community gossips

Networking and discussing Latest SAP Tech

For any further queries contact any one of the below community members :

SAP Stammtisch, Bengaluru
