This blog post will include highlights from this year and discuss common patterns in the challenges many of my trainees struggled with. I hope to assist others facing similar difficulties through this type of sharing.

## Information Overload

One of the most common challenges people faced was feeling overwhelmed by the vast amount of information available. If you’re feeling this way, you’re not alone. One of my mentees described it perfectly:

> “There’s just too much to learn, and every time I master one thing, three new things pop up.”
