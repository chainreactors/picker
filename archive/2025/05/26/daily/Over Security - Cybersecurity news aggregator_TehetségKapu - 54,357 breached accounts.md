# TehetségKapu

## What Happened

In March 2025, [almost 55k records were breached from the Hungarian education office website TehetségKapu](https://444.hu/2025/03/27/55-ezer-szemelyes-adat-magyar-diakok-tanarok-es-az-oktatasi-hivatal-dolgozoinak-informacioi-szivaroghattak-ki). The data was subsequently published to a popular hacking forum and included email addresses, names and usernames.
