### Tunneling trickery

[![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgJiEG_RLX8Ckh8pYCJx2VrhdC_ogcRGplckspXjMXnISY-U95SpsK3R-14IMl42666goPXFKKLUfV3uS2GcZkqL3eGS0Aa0UaD2S0r3JyB6NQxsH0rPLY4pvegqqxmvvz9NkfAbHtIIgiV68UkOB36ui8vnyTcVPkwAU5l7J1UEDXde_HaXMVzJf0ccg/s320/fraud-bridge.jpg)](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgJiEG_RLX8Ckh8pYCJx2VrhdC_ogcRGplckspXjMXnISY-U95SpsK3R-14IMl42666goPXFKKLUfV3uS2GcZkqL3eGS0Aa0UaD2S0r3JyB6NQxsH0rPLY4pvegqqxmvvz9NkfAbHtIIgiV68UkOB36ui8vnyTcVPkwAU5l7J1UEDXde_HaXMVzJf0ccg/s566/fraud-bridge.jpg)

I re-polished a [10y old project](https://github.com/stealth/fraud-bridge) that is one of the most complete tunneling solutions available for ICMP, ICMPv6, DNS over IP and DNS over IPv6 when it comes to setting up connectivity in restrictive environments. I added some fixes so it now properly also works behind NAT.
