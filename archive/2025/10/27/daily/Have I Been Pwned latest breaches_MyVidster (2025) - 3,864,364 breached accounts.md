# MyVidster (2025) Data Breach

## What Happened

In October 2025, [the data of almost 4M MyVidster users was posted to a public hacking forum](https://www.brinztech.com/breach-alerts/brinztech-myvidster-leak-exposes-4m-user-records-emails-usernames-phishing-risk/). Separate to the 2015 breach, this incident exposed usernames, email addresses and in a small number of cases, profile photos.
