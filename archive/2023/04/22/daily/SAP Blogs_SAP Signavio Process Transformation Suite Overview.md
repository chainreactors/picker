**Why Business Process Management and Transformation :**

**What is Business Process Management ( BPM ) ?**

* It is the practice of **discovering, analyzing, designing/modelling, monitoring, managing & optimizing** cross-functional processes to generate better business in any company’s operational success.

**Why is it important?**

* It is about managing business in the most efficient and effective way possible. It is to increase the quality of service, operational efficiency and agility, regulatory compliance and customer experience by establishing a common language in between IT and business in fast and scalable manner.

Change is the New Normal :

* Most of our SAP customers and leading organizations across all industries have built uncountable number of complex business processes

* With the increased pace of change, this complexity is likely to increase as business strive to deliver different products and service that should delivers great customer experience

* That’s why A digital transformation is needed for BPM continuously and at scale

* This will help to Achieve better business processes through best practices and collaboration and happier customers and employees by prioritizing their experience throughout your process change

**Manage your digital transformation continuously and at scale with the help of SAP Signavio**

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/04/Picture1-56.png)

**SAP Signavio Introduction and Key benefits :**

**SAP Signavio : A cloud-based process transformation suite that gives companies the ability to understand, improve and transform all of their (end-to-end)** **business processes, fast and at scale across SAP and non-SAP solutions.**

**Key benefits**

* **Align across the organization -** Model, govern, share, and collaborate on processes and Involve all stakeholders

* **Reduce time to insight -** Discover and leverage your data to identify process improvements areas based on problems and inconsistencies in current BPM

* **Bring experience and process excellence together​ -** Combine outside-in and inside-out experience to translate customer, supplier, and employee experience to operational reality

* **Increase agility and reduce time to action​ -** Identify and execute improvement opportunities with delivered configurations, metrics, and tailored recommendations.
