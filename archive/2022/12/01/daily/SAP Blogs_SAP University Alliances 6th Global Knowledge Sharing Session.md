SAP University Alliances launched regular Global Knowledge Sharing Sessions for our Academic Community in 2021.

On November 28th we’ve organised the 6th Session with the following Agenda:

1. Dr. Michael Nuernberg, VP, SAP University Alliances Regional Director, “**Business Processes Open Lectures**”

2. Dr. Igor Belousov, SAP University Alliances Content Delivery Director, “**Updates from SAP TechEd 2022**”

3. Heather Matthews, SAP University Alliances Global Programs Director, **"UA portfolio update: Learning Pathways and New Curricula"**

4. **Q&A**

The links to the Session´s materials are below:

* [Recordings](https://sapvideoa35699dc5.hana.ondemand.com/?entry_id=1_yhcxi1m8 "Recordings")

* [Presentation 1](https://d.dam.sap.com/a/WumpJbi/2022-11-28_SAP-UA-GKSS_MN_BusinessProcessesOpenLecture.pdf?rc=10)

* [Presentation 2](https://d.dam.sap.com/a/u8ckyzD/SAP%20TechEd%202022%20overview.pdf?rc=10)

* [Presentation 3](https://d.dam.sap.com/a/xDKyYou/UA_Portfolio_Update_Nov28-2002.pdf?rc=10)

Next Global Knowledge Sharing Session will happen in Q1 2023. Stay tuned!
