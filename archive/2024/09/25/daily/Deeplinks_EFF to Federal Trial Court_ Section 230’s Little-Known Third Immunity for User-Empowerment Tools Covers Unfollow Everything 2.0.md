*Update: In a [written order on November 22, 2024](https://www.courthousenews.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/zuckerman-vs-meta-order-dismiss-granted.pdf), the district court judge granted Meta's motion to dismiss without prejudice, meaning that Prof. Zuckerman may refile his case at a later date. The judge ruled that the case wasn't ripe for consideration because Prof. Zuckerman hasn't built the Unfollow Everything 2.0 tool yet.*

EFF along with the ACLU of Northern California and the Center for Democracy & Technology filed an [amicus brief](https://www.eff.org/document/eff-aclu-norcal-cdt-amicus-brief-zuckerman-v-meta-nd-cal) in a federal trial court in California in support of a college professor who fears being sued by Meta for developing a tool that allows Facebook users to easily clear out their News Feed.
