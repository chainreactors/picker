I recorded a [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PJ5oluVlEb8) for my diary entry "[Detecting (Malicious) OneNote Files](https://isc.sans.edu/diary/Detecting%20%28Malicious%29%20OneNote%20Files/29494)".

It shows how I familiarized myzelf with the .one file format, enough to know how to extract embedded files, wrote a tool ([onedump.py](https://blog.didierstevens.com/2023/01/22/new-tool-onedump-py/)) and take a look at detection rules.
