**June 15th we are organizing the second edition of the Developer Day. It will be an inspiring afternoon completely dedicated to SAP technology.** **Prepare to dive into the latest trends, tips and innovations that are relevant to your goals and personal growth.**

## Who are we?

A group of motivated developers, passionate about SAP, innovation, technology and developing awesome applications! With this event we want to bring together people with a similar mindset, looking to explore SAP’s latest offerings and gain new insights.

## What to expect?

* Arrive at 12:30 PM for lunch and **meet your fellow attendees**.

* At 1:30 PM we’ll kick off the afternoon with a **keynote** that will inspire you for the rest of the program.

* After the keynote, we’ll split up in different **tracks**: ABAP & S/4, BTP, Integration & low code.

* At 3:00 PM we'll hold a break where you can **talk with the experts**, grab a drink on the terrace or even participate in a **tour of the brewery**

* At 5:30 PM we'll call it a day, grab a **drink** and a bite to**eat** and start mingling!

## Discover the full program

Discover the full line-up and program [here](https://www.flexso.com/uploads/files/Events/2023_Dev-Day-Program-v2.pdf)!

## Practical information

**Date:**June 15, from 12:30 till 19:30

**Location:** Brewery The Musketeers - Reepstraat 208, 9170 Sint-Gillis-Waas

## Who can attend?

The Developer Day is free to attend for every SAP developer looking to stay up-to-date with the latest sap technologies and ready to mingle with like-minded spirits. Places are limited though, so register now and secure your spot![**Reserve your spot now**](https://www.flexso.com/en/events/developerday-23)**.**

We hope to see you there!
