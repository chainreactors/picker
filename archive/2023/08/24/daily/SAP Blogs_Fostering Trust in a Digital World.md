In early 2023, the Chief Trust Office published our first Annual Message titled “Fostering Trust in a Digital World”. Our goal for the publication of the Annual Message was to share our perspective and commitment to trust, how we shape and deliver on customer commitments, and how we foster trust across our ecosystem with our stakeholders.

We have developed a short video, where Elena Kvochko, Chief Trust Officer at SAP, provides an overview of the key concepts of the Annual Message. Providing a visual representation of our message is an avenue the Chief Trust Office wanted to explore to improve accessibility and better deliver on our commitment to transparency.

Please enjoy and share the video below.

To read the complete “Fostering Trust in a Digital World”, click [here](https://support.sap.com/en/my-support/trust-center/tools-information.html?anchorId=34d12b4688dfde59bc35732e139440a6). (log-in required)
