# [Kaitai Struct WebIDE](/forums/diary/Kaitai%2BStruct%2BWebIDE/32422/)

**Published**: 2025-10-26. **Last Updated**: 2025-10-26 16:01:53 UTC
**by** [Didier Stevens](/handler_list.html#didier-stevens) (Version: 1)

[0 comment(s)](/diary/Kaitai%2BStruct%2BWebIDE/32422/#comments)

When I have a binary file to analyze, I often use tools like 010 Editor or format-bytes.py (a tool I develop). Sometimes I also use [Kaitai Struct](https://kaitai.io/).

What I did not know, is that Kaitai Struct also has a [WebIDE](https://ide.kaitai.io/), that allows you to parse all kinds of binary file formats in your browser.

I learned this during a [presentation of the KaiTai struct developers](https://youtu.be/SC2zIli8MNA) at Hack.lu this week.

The demo of the WebIDE starts around [17:31 in the video](https://youtu.be/SC2zIli8MNA?t=1051).
