Hi SAP Community!

I'm glad to announce that I'll be delivering the SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement Onboarding sessions next week, from 9 to 11 of May.

### Background Context

SAP Emarsys is an effective and straight-to-the-point solution that empowers marketers to increase their customer engagement capabilities.

This 3-day Live Session will present a first onboarding and an overview of SAP Emarsys for beginner-level audiences.

### What is it about?

The SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement Onboarding sessions will offer you a hands-on experience through the main functionalities. You will be guided by a real-life Use Case to which a solution will be designed – and built by you throughout the exercises, step by step.

### Who is it for?

This live session is designed for Marketing Managers, Consultants, Partners, and other roles involved in day-to-day marketing operations.

### What does it offer?

This is a 3-day (6 hours in total) instructor-led hands-on session. We offer you:

* Access to a training account (after registration)

* An instructor to answer course-related questions

* Lots of interactivity, to keep everyone engaged

### How can you book it?

This live session is available on the [SAP Learning Hub](https://learninghub.sap.com/), with an active subscription.
