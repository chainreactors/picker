First of all, I’d like to let you know that the blog is currently being updated. I’ve been making a few changes over the past few hours, as I felt it was time to refresh the style—partly inspired by some feedback I’ve received over time, and partly because I wanted to give the blog a new look.

Regarding the topics for tonight’s live stream, I’ve chosen two themes that are not overly technical.

The first topic will involve discussing recent statements made by Minister Crosetto during a hearing on the subject of a “cyber armed force.” There are several interesting points that I’d like to explore and spark some reflections on. You can find more information about it here:

* <https://formiche.net/2025/01/cyber-difesa-e-forza-audizione-crosetto/#content>.
* <https://www.difesa.it/primopiano/audizione-del-ministro-crosetto-sulla-difesa-cibernetica/63305.html>

The second topic is a spin-off from Monday’s upcoming podcast, titled *“How Much Should a CISO Know?”* I’ve enjoyed reading various opinions on our Telegram group following the survey we conducted, and I’ll be discussing those during the live stream.

The podcast itself is progressing well—Andrea and I recorded two additional episodes on this subject, totaling 80 minutes of conversation, which will be released in two parts.

For those joining, I’ll see you live tonight at 9:30 PM on Twitch!
