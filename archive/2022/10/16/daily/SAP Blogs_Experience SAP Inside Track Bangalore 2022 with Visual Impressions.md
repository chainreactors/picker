After weeks of planning and preparation, which resulted in a successful [SAP Inside Track Bangalore 2022 Event](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/09/16/sap-inside-track-bengaluru-oct-1st-2022/), we have many great reasons to put together post-event content. After all, it focused on significant insights, novel ideas, and innovative concepts you won't quickly forget.

This was a fantastic [event](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/09/16/sap-inside-track-bengaluru-oct-1st-2022/), specifically the breakout meetings or gatherings. The presenters made the sessions informative and exciting, and the knowledge they provided was helpful for attendees.

[SAP Labs India](https://www.sap.com/india/about/labs-india.html) Bangalore campus hosted [SAP Inside Track 2022 Event](https://blogs.sap.com/2022/09/16/sap-inside-track-bengaluru-oct-1st-2022/). It is the fastest-growing subsidiary of [SAP](https://www.sap.com/india/index.html), a multinational company that provides customers with world-class business solutions.

## Registration

Event Day started on a rainy morning with unpredictable weather. Still, somehow, we (maheshkumar.palavalli) could all reach one hour before the event began. Soon we were ready with the stylish lanyard event badges and a bag of sponsor's promotional giveaways and event gifts.

After a while, Attendees started arriving at the event registration counter, where we welcomed them by providing them with an event badge and goodies.

## Lunch & Networking

SAP Labs India organized excellent Lunch and evening snacks, selected hot and cold beverages, premixes, variety of cookies and fruits. It was arranged in world-class Cafe 5, the cafeteria with different food varieties and options.

Everyone enjoyed the delicious food, and great to catch up with friends to strengthen relationships. Many SAP technology talks at the table went on as ideas and discussions took over the conversation about multiple SAP topics.

Specifically, I have met with my old friends with new excitement and successful product stories.

## Chat with SAP Mentor & SAP Champion

After the Sessions, we had a fireside chat with [SAP Mentor](https://community.sap.com/programs/influencer-programs/mentors) - krishnakishor.kammaje2 to share their journey in SAP Community and also gave tips to the attendees on how to engage in community activity.

ruthvik.chowdary2 mentioned that ***to become an******[SAP Champion](https://community.sap.com/programs/influencer-programs/champions), you need to be active in LinkedIn and [SAP Community](https://community.sap.com/) by writing [blogs](https://blogs.sap.com/) and participating in [Community events](https://groups.community.sap.com/t5/events/ct-p/events).***

krishnakishor.kammaje2 recognized his community contributions and an opening for more opportunities to continue his work.
