Earlier this year, the Oversight Board [announced a review of three cases](https://www.oversightboard.com/news/oversight-board-announces-new-cases-on-posts-that-include-from-the-river-to-the-sea/) involving different pieces of content on Facebook that contained the phrase “From the River to the Sea.” EFF [submitted to the consultation](https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2024/05/eff-submission-oversight-board-posts-include-river-sea) urging Meta to make individualized moderation decisions on this content rather than a blanket ban as the phrase can be a historical call for Palestinian liberation and not an incitement of hatred in violation with Meta’s community standards.

We’re happy to see that the Oversight Board agreed.
