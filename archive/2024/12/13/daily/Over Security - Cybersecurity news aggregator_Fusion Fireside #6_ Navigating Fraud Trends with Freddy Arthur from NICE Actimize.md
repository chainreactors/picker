## Fusion Fireside #6: Navigating Fraud Trends with Freddy Arthur from NICE Actimize

12 December 2024

![](https://www.threatfabric.com/hubfs/TF_FusionFireside_FreddyArthur.png)

### Jump to

Tune in to the next episode of our Fusion Fireside series, a series about the dynamic world of Online Fraud and Threat Intelligence! In this episode, Eward Driehuis will be joined by Freddy Arthur, the Fraud Strategy Leader for EMEA at NICE Actimize.

##

## Meet Freddy Arthur

Freddy Arthur brings over a decade of experience in helping organisations tackle complex customer decision-making challenges through the innovative use of technology, data, and processes. As the Fraud Strategy Leader for EMEA at NICE Actimize, Freddy specialises in identity, fraud, and financial crime domains. His passion lies in enabling clients to leverage digital channels to deliver exceptional customer experiences while maintaining robust fraud prevention and detection strategies. Freddy has collaborated with some of the world's largest financial institutions and has a rich consultancy background spanning Central Government, Telco, and Gaming industries.

## What to Expect in this Episode

In this episode, Freddy and Eward will explore a range of compelling topics, including:

* **Freddy's Journey into Fraud Prevention**: How did Freddy get started in this field, and what keeps him passionate about it?

* **Emerging Fraud Trends**: What are the latest fraud methods and tactics Freddy is seeing across different clients?

* **The Impact of PSR**: How does Freddy view the upcoming Payment Services Regulation (PSR), and will the rest of the world follow suit?

* **Memorable Cases**: Freddy shares a case that left a lasting impression on him.

* **Evolution of Fraud Prevention**: How has the landscape of fraud prevention changed over the years?

* **The Concept of Fraud Fusion**: Is integrating fraud and threat intelligence a viable approach to becoming more proactive in fraud prevention?

* **Looking into the Future**: Freddy gives us a glimpse into his "crystal ball" to predict what's coming next in the world of fraud prevention.

Stay tuned for an engaging and informative discussion that promises to provide valuable insights and practical advice for anyone involved in fraud prevention and threat intelligence.
