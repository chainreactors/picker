**by** [Didier Stevens](/handler_list.html#didier-stevens) (Version: 1)

[0 comment(s)](/diary/YARA%2B453%2BRelease/31976/#comments)

[YARA 4.5.3](https://github.com/VirusTotal/yara/releases/tag/v4.5.3) was released with 5 bugfixes.

I want to take this as an opportunity to remind you that YARA is to be replaced with [YARA-X](https://virustotal.github.io/yara-x/), a rewrite in Rust.

[YARA-X is already powering VirusTotal](https://virustotal.github.io/yara-x/blog/virustotal-moves-to-yara-x/).
