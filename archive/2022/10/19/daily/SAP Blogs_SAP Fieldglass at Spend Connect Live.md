Spend Connect Live kicks off Monday, October 24th in Dallas, Texas featuring content across all spend categories, including external workforce! This is the first time since 2019 that our community will be brought together and I’m so excited for what we have to offer! From demos, solution adoption, and roadmap sessions to a community networking event planned just for the Fieldglass community, there will be many ways to engage and network at the event!

If you have not yet registered, there’s still to join us October 24-26th at the Sheraton Dallas Hotel. Be sure to [register here](https://go2.events.sap.com/SpendConnectLive2022/) to confirm your attendance and take advantage of all that we have to offer.

In addition to mainstage sessions where you will hear from Accenture about their Services Procurement journey and learn more about a new quarterly trends feature that will be available with the November release, I personally suggest attending the following:
