# Animeify

## What Happened

In October 2021, the now defunct Arabic language Anime website Animeify suffered a data breach [that was later redistributed as part of a larger corpus of data](https://cybernews.com/security/billions-passwords-credentials-leaked-mother-of-all-breaches/). The data included 808k unique email addresses along with names, usernames, genders and plain text passwords.
