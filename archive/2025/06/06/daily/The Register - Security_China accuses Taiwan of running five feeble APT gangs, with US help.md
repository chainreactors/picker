## The authors who claimed America hacked itself to discredit Beijing are back with another report

![icon](/design_picker/d518b499f8a6e2c65d4d8c49aca8299d54b03012/graphics/icon/vulture_red.svg)
[Simon Sharwood](/Author/Simon-Sharwood "Read more by this author")

04:49 UTC

![](/design_picker/d2e337b97204af4aa34dda04c4e5d56d954b216f/graphics/icons/social_share_icon.svg)

China’s National Computer Virus Emergency Response Center on Thursday published a report in which it claims Taiwan targeted it with a years-long cyber offensive, backed by the USA, but which was so feeble Beijing complains compared it to an ant trying to shake a tree .

In a [report](https://www.cverc.org.cn/head/zhaiyao/Investigation_report_on_Cyberattacks_launched_by_Taiwan_ICEFCOM_EN.pdf) [PDF] titled “Operation Futile: Investigation report on Cyberattacks launched by ICEFCOM of Taiwan and its affiliated [advanced persistent threat] APT actors”.

> The clumsy performance of the hacker groups is as ridiculous as an ant trying to shake a tree

“ICEFOM” is the acronym for Taiwan’s Information, Communications and Electronic Force Command, which the contested territory [created](https://nspp.mofa.gov.tw/nsppe/content_tt.php?unit=2&post=117794) in 2017.
