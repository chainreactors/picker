SAP Business One has been around for over two decades and is here to stay. With great functional scope, very flexible extensibility and customization options, various localizations and languages, two database server options, flexible deployment models and a Web Client - every SME can leverage this solution for their business needs - as 75,000 customers worldwide already do.

This growing success is resulting of knowledge, experience, and continuous dialog with SAP Business One partners and customers.

Therefore, we are happy to announce the new blog series "SAP Business One Product Management Insights", every month, one of the product management team members will publish a blog about her or his SAP Business One area of expertise, and will share their insights and knowledge about a functionality, concept, or best practice.

All the blogs will be available under this user tag: [b1pminsights](https://blogs.sap.com/tag/b1pminsights/) . If you wish to receive notification whenever a new blog in the series in published, please follow this blog.

Hope you'll find these blogs valuable!
