In [*Murthy v. Missouri*](https://www.supremecourt.gov/opinions/23pdf/23-411_3dq3.pdf), the U.S. Supreme Court did not answer the important First Amendment question before it—how does one distinguish permissible from impermissible government communications with social media platforms about the speech they publish?
