# Cultura

## What Happened

In September 2024, [French retailer Cultura was the victim of a cyber attack they attributed to an external IT service provider](https://therecord.media/france-retailers-hacked-confirm-cyberattack). The resultant data breach included almost 1.5M unique email addresses along with names, phone numbers, physical addresses and orders. Cultura advised that all affected customers had been notified about the incident.
