Governments increasingly rely on algorithmic systems to support consequential assessments and determinations about people’s lives, from judging eligibility for social assistance to trying to predict crime and criminals. [Latin America](https://ia.derechosdigitales.org/en/) is [no exception](https://notmy.ai/mapping-of-projects/). With the use of artificial intelligence (AI) posing human rights challenges in the region, EFF released today the report *[Inter-American Standards and State Use of AI for Rights-Affecting Determinations in Latin America: Human Rights Implications and Operational Framework](https://www.eff.org/pages/human-rights-standards-government-use-ai-latin-america)*.
