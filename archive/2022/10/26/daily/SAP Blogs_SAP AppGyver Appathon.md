With [#AppGyver](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/?keywords=appgyver&highlightedUpdateUrns=urn%3Ali%3Aactivity%3A6970642696579256320), [#SAP](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/?keywords=sap&highlightedUpdateUrns=urn%3Ali%3Aactivity%3A6970642696579256320) has a solution to involve citizen developers. ![:mobile_phone_with_arrow:](/html/@67FB88826172B82A8F2DCA09C9D72024/emoticons/1f4f2.png ":mobile_phone_with_arrow:")

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2022/10/Cover-1-1.jpg)

You want to learn how to do it? My colleagues will introduce you to [#SAP](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/?keywords=sap&highlightedUpdateUrns=urn%3Ali%3Aactivity%3A6975712608922263552) [#AppGyver](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/hashtag/?keywords=appgyver&highlightedUpdateUrns=urn%3Ali%3Aactivity%3A6975712608922263552) and everyone can build their own app. On 27th October 2022 from 1pm to 4pm (UTC+2).

Click here to register: [Registration | Appathon SAP AppGyver](https://webinars.sap.com/de/appathon-sap-appgyver/de/registration.aspx)

We look forward to seeing you there. ![:smiling_face_with_sunglasses:](/html/@21F944EE123330485E3AFC54A3C0DB62/emoticons/1f60e.png ":smiling_face_with_sunglasses:")

P.S. Please don't forget to get the free community version. Trying and testing beforehand is explicitly allowed.

Here you have the SAP Developer mission: [Create an Application with SAP AppGyver | Tutorials for SAP Developers](https://developers.sap.com/tutorials/appgyver-create-application.html)

Here you get your free to use AppGyver community version: [SAP AppGyver | visuelle Low-Code-Programmierung | SAP](https://www.sap.com/germany/products/technology-platform/appgyver.html)
