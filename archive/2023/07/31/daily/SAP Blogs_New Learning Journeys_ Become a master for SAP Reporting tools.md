Digitalization is growing with pretty high rhythms, and learning could not be an exception on this phaenomenon. Learners try to find new ways for upskilling with the minimum cost. SAP Product Learning organization has anticipated this need, and works on this direction. Our main goal is to create quality Learning Journeys in digital format, for different audiences and roles, and with high learner experience.

![](/legacyfs/online/storage/blog_attachments/2023/07/FeeQJPEWIAELJGb-scaled.jpg)

Several learning journeys on SAP reporting tools are available for free in [**learning.sap.com**](http://learning.sap.com/). Learners are able to build their knowledge not only with textual and graphical information, but with many videos and engaging interactions such as simulations, hot maps, knowledge checks, which support their understanding of the required concepts.

**Who is this learning journey for?**

+ Are you a Reporting Consultant  or Technical Consultant, wanting to master some of the essential features of SAP Crystal Reports, Business Objects, SAP Analytics Cloud?

+ Are you interested in learning how to design, filter, and plan your reports ?

+ Do you want to learn how to create a great user experience on how to build reports?

+ Are you curious about integration with other products?

If the answer to the above questions is yes, you are in the right place! Learn in structured units and lessons at your own pace.
