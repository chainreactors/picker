# Cybersecurity regulation stepping up

## Understanding new NIS2, DORA, and Tiber-EU legislation is essential to improving IT security, explains SANS

[Annaliese Ingrams](/Author/Annaliese-Ingrams "Read more by this author")

Mon 9 Sep 2024  //
09:00 UTC

![](/design_picker/d2e337b97204af4aa34dda04c4e5d56d954b216f/graphics/icons/social_share_icon.svg)

Webinar As cybersecurity regulations tighten, organisations face new challenges that require more than just compliance checklists.

The latest legislative frameworks - NIS2, DORA, and Tiber-EU - demand strategic shifts in how IT security operates.

Navigating these complex regulations is increasingly critical, especially as businesses become more globally interconnected. For cybersecurity professionals, this means staying informed and prepared to meet these new standards.

[Join us](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/30374/cybersecurity-regulation-steps-up-with-nis2-dora-and-tiber-eu) on September 16, 2024, at 11:00 AM BST (12:00 PM CEST) for an exclusive webinar where Chris Dale, SANS Principal Instructor, will break down the implications of NIS2, DORA, and Tiber-EU. You'll gain:

*- In-depth knowledge of the NIS2, DORA, and Tiber-EU regulatory frameworks.*

*- Strategic insights into how these regulations will shape the IT security landscape.*

*- Best practices for implementing cyber resilience and overcoming challenges.*

*- Exclusive survey findings to enhance your organization's defence against cyber threats.*

Don't miss this opportunity to equip yourself with the knowledge and tools needed to stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity regulation.

[Register for the webinar by clicking here](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/30374/cybersecurity-regulation-steps-up-with-nis2-dora-and-tiber-eu).
