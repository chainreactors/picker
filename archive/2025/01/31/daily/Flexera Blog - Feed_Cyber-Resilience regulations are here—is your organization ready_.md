# Cyber-Resilience regulations are here—is your organization ready?

[Jennifer Kuvlesky](https://www.flexera.com/blog/author/jennifer-kuvlesky/ "Jennifer Kuvlesky")

January 29, 2025

![Cyber-Resilience regulations are here—is your organization ready?](https://www.flexera.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/1839-Blog-header-images-set2-07-915x478.png)

![Flexera logo](https://www.flexera.com/blog/wp-content/themes/f1/assets/images/logo-flexera.png)

![Image: Cyber-Resilience regulations are here—is your organization ready?](https://www.flexera.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/1839-Blog-header-images-set2-07.png)

Recent regulations require organizations to demonstrate they are resilient to cyber-attacks. These regulations impact regulated organizations and those in the supply chain, as modern threat actors target vulnerabilities across the entire ecosystem.

Cybersecurity has become a boardroom, high-priority agenda item with heightened legal exposure, increases in supply chain attacks, and reported vulnerabilities.
