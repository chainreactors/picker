# Discover the future of Linux security

## Explore open source strategies to safeguard critical systems and data

[Annaliese Ingrams](/Author/Annaliese-Ingrams "Read more by this author")

Mon 2 Dec 2024  //
14:45 UTC

![](/design_picker/d2e337b97204af4aa34dda04c4e5d56d954b216f/graphics/icons/social_share_icon.svg)

Webinar Linux security is a component that sits at the heart of today's IT landscape.

On December 10th at 10am PT/1pm ET, Red Hat invites IT professionals to [State of Linux Security Symposium 2024](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/32941/the-state-of-linux-security-symposium-2024) - an event that offers practical strategies for securing Linux environments.

The symposium covers:

- **Building blocks of security** – Core principles to enhance your defenses.

- **Securing the supply chain** – Proven methods to protect against vulnerabilities.

- **The RHEL advantage** – Why Red Hat Enterprise Linux is the trusted choice for leading organizations worldwide.

With insights drawn from real-world use cases and demonstrations, Red Hat will showcase its activity within the Linux ecosystem over the course of six sessions, emphasizing how open collaboration with customers can drive innovation and robust security solutions.

Join IT leaders from industries like government, finance, and telecommunications as they explore strategies to safeguard critical systems and data.

This event is your chance to learn from the experts and gain actionable insights to strengthen your Linux infrastructure.

[Sign up](https://whitepapers.theregister.com/paper/view/32941/the-state-of-linux-security-symposium-2024) to access these webinars here.
