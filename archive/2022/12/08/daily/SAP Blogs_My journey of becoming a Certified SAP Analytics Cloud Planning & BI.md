In my role as a Customer Success Partner at [SAP](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sap/) my main task is to help drive adoption of SAP Analytics Cloud, and I’m frequently asked for good learning resources. Over the years I’ve collected several great, often free, resources that I would like to share with you. I originally published this post  here: [Master SAP Analytics Cloud | LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/master-sap-analytics-cloud-ramy-salem/?trackingId=4VIYKJFcQVSFVj6YoACkIA%3D%3D)

Getting Start!

OpenSAP is SAP's learning platform for everyone interested in learning about SAP's latest innovations, and they have a good selection of SAP Analytics Cloud related coursesat no charge.

**OpenSAP-Courses: -**

* (SAC01) Intelligent Decisions with SAP Analytics Cloud: <https://open.sap.com/courses/sac1>

This course focuses on business intelligence, augmented analytics capabilities, and financial planning.

![No alt text provided for this image](https://media-exp1.licdn.com/dms/image/D5612AQE7PRS1rTf8iA/article-inline_image-shrink_1500_2232/0/1669004691760?e=1675296000&v=beta&t=YRmCJ-ncFlsgrK_ikSKy4zVq-kHEh9BZJZexyawaedw)

* (SAC02) SAP Analytics Cloud – Authentication and Data Connectivity  <https://open.sap.com/courses/sac2>

This course is tailored to system admins and helps them learn about data sources and connectivity, as well as authentication options, for both on-premises and cloud systems.

![No alt text provided for this image](https://media-exp1.licdn.com/dms/image/D5612AQHUndwbvCog0w/article-inline_image-shrink_1500_2232/0/1669004758075?e=1675296000&v=beta&t=ZIDbN4hp71Z-GIUKEd4obUwAboBZ8cBX_sa6y4dBS9o)

* (SAC03) Planning with SAP Analytics Cloud: <https://open.sap.com/courses/sac3>

One of the major features of SAP Analytics Cloud is its planning capabilities, this course gives an insight into how you can deploy those.

* (SAC04) Planning with SAP Analytics Cloud – Advanced Topics: <https://open.sap.com/courses/sac4>

The final OpenSAP course is about the advanced planning capabilities of SAP Analytics Cloud. It includes the new modelling environment, expert use-cases for data actions and advanced formulas.

**Did you know that SAP Analytics Cloud comes with pre-built content?**

The Business Contents are designed to provide you with a quick and easy starting point for your analytics scenarios. It includes predefined Stories, Dashboards and Data models that are tailored to existing SAP data sources. You can easily connect the content to your own data and get started with your Analytics Journey in SAP Analytics Cloud.
