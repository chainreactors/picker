**Agenda**

1.**【论文分享****】**Akamai DNS: Providing Authoritative Answers to the World's Queriess

* **Presenter:**张丰露
* **Conference:** SIGCOMM'20
* **Authors:**

  Kyle Schomp, Onkar Bhardwaj, Eymen Kurdoglu, Mashooq Muhaimen, Ramesh K. Sitaraman
* **Abstract:**

   This paper presents the design of Akamai DNS authoritative sevices, including load balancing mechisiams, failure resiliency, and attack resiliency. The paper also provides evaluation of the above mechisiams.
* **Link to paper:**

  https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3387514.3405881

2.**【论文分享】**autofz: Automated Fuzzer Composition at Runtime

* **Presenter:**高泽豫
* **Conference:**USENIX Security'23
* **Authors:** Yu-Fu Fu, Jaehyuk Lee, Taesoo Kim
* **Abstract:**

   This paper presents a design that automated, non-intrusive meta-fuzzer that can maximize the benefits of existing state-of-the-art fuzzers via dynamic composition.
* **Link to paper:**

  https://gts3.org/assets/papers/2023/fu:autofz.pdf
