## Check out the star-studded speaker-line-up for the much-awaited cybersecurity conference.

Hello hacker

We are happy to share the value-packed speaker line-up of [**IWCON2022**](https://iwcon.live/) — the virtual Cybersecurity Conference and Networking Event organized by InfoSec WriteUps Publication.

Featuring amazing speakers like -

1. [**Gabrielle Hempel**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAzkH3YBQ5ZAEIs8A-3y7-JVmKD1RCPFfkE) on “Threat hunting in cloud environments” 🌩️
2. [**Luke (hakluke) Stephens**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAACcK9ewBCyIzphohk027wOvn6V6sdXUpumo) on “How I used recon techniques to identify a prolific scammer.” 👊
3. [**Inti De Ceukelaire**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAt-ZUoBBCX0J4-Pl7WCcOvl4ME74Y4rCRg)on “Reading RFCs for hacking”🔥
4. [**Phillip Wylie**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAC83qsBY7Bg0BcjiVQUYHj6TbW_8CYqd1s) on “API security through external attack surface management.” 😍
5. [**Dhiyaneshwaran B.**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAACBIe_0BL8bJJu9xFIlcnBaZrmLaWFq1P0g) on “Hacking cloud: for fun and profit” 😁
6. [**Ahsan Khan**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAACJtp7UBxCZMZhHrf0cCx0EJGIoyWZzyL1s) on “My approach to accessing admin panels” 💪
7. [**Florian Hansemann**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAACSpRcYBQlDQ0HUbpofn6aBJKEgiBg61lb8) on “Most common ways to hack companies in under 4 hours” 🔥🔥
8. **Blake McDermott** on “The good, the bad and the ugly of IIS modules” 👊
9. **John O.** on “Impact security at scale” 💪🏻
10. **Mohammed Diaa** (head of workflows, [Trickest](https://www.linkedin.com/company/trickest/)) on “Method vs Madness — Developing Flexible Automation Workflows.” 🔥
11. [**Shashank** .](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAABT0gDUBq047uyzov2eicn0gC5wrb5b5kes) (CEO, [CredShields](https://www.linkedin.com/company/credshields/)) on “Starting into smart contract security” 🤩
12. [**Jonathan Leitschuh**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAA0SLMUBScBUspIv0-LQ1ecAwsqt5l81eG4)on “Scaling the security researcher to eliminate OSS vulnerabilities once and for all” 😍
13. [**Youssef Sammouda**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAADNRVM4BNawdtQP-EPsUA0mHHY8doD_gfFk)on “Account hijacking featuring OAuth and javascript” 🤩
14. [**Majd Aldeen Atiyat**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAS6JjEBZNAdQqkzkm3z9bzlo7chYAhqYPg) (aka Th3G3nt3lman) on “My journey in bug bounty & unique findings”
15. [**Orwa Atiyat**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAC5koR0B7lTV3JSlKCfwG4XmaDAzoVf20Ds) on “Recon skills and tips” 🙌
16. [**Yarden Shafir**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAwNiOYBvKTE1o3yyPD38Nsg0gAuJ4gbcWA) on “The Researcher’s Toolbox” 🔥

[**Check the full speaker line-up, and book your seats today**](https://iwcon.live/) (Happening LIVE on 17–18 Dec, 6 PM IST onwards).
