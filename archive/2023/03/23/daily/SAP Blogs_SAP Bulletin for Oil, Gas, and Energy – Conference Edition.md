**Dear SAP Oil, Gas, and Energy Community,**

I am glad to share the [SAP Bulletin for Oil, Gas, and Energy - Special Conference Edition](https://www.sap.com/documents/2023/03/e4018c97-687e-0010-bca6-c68f7e60039b.html) with you. In this edition, you will find an overview and highlights of this year's SAP International Conference for [Energy](https://tacinsights.eventsair.com/cmspreview/international-sap-conference-for-energy/) and [Utilities](https://tacinsights.eventsair.com/international-sap-conference-for-utilities), which will take place in Basel, Switzerland, from 18-20 April 2023 and address a wide variety of topics that can help your company thrive in a time of energy transition such as **Asset Management, Customer, Sustainable Energy Transition, New Business and Processes, Oil, Gas & Energy, Renewable Power Generation, SAP Utilities Core including SAP S/4HANA, Sustainability,** and **Water.**
