# Qraved

## What Happened

In July 2021, the Indonesian restaurant website Qraved suffered a data breach [that was later redistributed as part of a larger corpus of data](https://cybernews.com/security/billions-passwords-credentials-leaked-mother-of-all-breaches/). The breach exposed almost 1M unique email addresses along with names, phone numbers, dates of birth and passwords stored as MD5 hashes.
